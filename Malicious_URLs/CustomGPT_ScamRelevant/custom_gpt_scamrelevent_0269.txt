### 5. **Enhanced Risk Management for Investors**:

* **Risk assessment tools**: AI can help investors assess the **risk factors** associated with a particular property or area by analyzing **economic shifts**, **natural disaster risks**, or **local crime rates**. This data would be crucial for **real estate investors** looking to make decisions based on long-term growth projections.
* **Automated valuation models (AVMs)**: AI-powered AVMs will continue to improve in assessing the **true value** of a property based on hundreds of variables beyond just recent sales in the area, including future development plans, neighborhood sentiment, and even the local real estate “mood.”

### 6. **Impact on Housing Affordability**:

* **AI to address affordability**: AI could be used to propose **innovative solutions** for the **housing affordability crisis** in Florida. Additionally, AI might identify which neighborhoods could see the biggest increase in **demand** and **value**, which could inform policy decisions around where to focus affordable housing efforts.

### 7. **Data-Driven Policy Changes**:

* **Government and urban planning**: AI can assist policymakers in better understanding **housing trends** and **population dynamics**, leading to more informed decisions regarding **zoning laws**, **housing developments**, and **infrastructure investments**.
