* **Dividends**: A portion of a company’s earnings that is paid to shareholders. Understanding dividends can help you choose income-generating investments.
* **EPS (Earnings Per Share)**: A company's profit divided by the number of its outstanding shares, which is crucial for understanding a company’s profitability.
* **Beta**: A measure of a stock’s volatility in relation to the overall market. A beta higher than 1 means the stock is more volatile than the market.
* **Revenue, Gross Profit, and Net Income**: These are basic indicators of a company’s financial health. Understanding how they relate to each other will help you evaluate a company’s performance.
