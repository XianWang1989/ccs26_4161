⏱️ **Impact:** Saves providers and staff hours per week spent on PAs.

---

### 5️⃣ **Faster Updates of Formularies**

Currently, **formularies update frequently**, requiring manual tracking.
AI will:

* Continuously scrape and parse **insurer and PBM sites for formulary changes**.
* Update EHR-integrated formulary databases **without manual intervention**.

📌 **Impact:** Keeps formulary data current, reducing prescription rejections due to outdated information.

---

### 6️⃣ **Patient-Facing Tools Will Emerge**

Patients will:

* Use AI chatbots to check **if a drug is covered under their plan** before visiting the doctor.
* Receive **real-time alerts if formulary status changes** (e.g., a drug moves to a non-preferred tier).

🧑‍⚕️ **Impact:** Reduces the back-and-forth between patients and clinics over coverage questions.
