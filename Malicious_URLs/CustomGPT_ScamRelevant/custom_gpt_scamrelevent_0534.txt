* **Prescription Drugs** → Rigorously tested, FDA-regulated, require a doctor’s prescription.

* Supplements and medications can interact in **three main ways**:

  2. **Pharmacodynamic** → One changes the effect of the other at the target site.
  3. **Additive Effects** → Combining similar effects can cause overdose or toxicity.
* Example: Fish oil + warfarin = higher bleeding risk.
