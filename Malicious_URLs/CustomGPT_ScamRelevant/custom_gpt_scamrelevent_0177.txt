✅ **Timeframes:**

* **1-minute to 1-hour** = short-term scalping context.
* **4-hour to daily** = swing trading context.
* **Weekly/monthly** = macro cycle context.

✅ **Support and Resistance:**

* Support: price level where buyers step in.
* Resistance: price level where sellers step in.

✅ **Indicators Used Here:**

* **RSI:**

  * > 70 = overbought (potential pullback zone).

  * Rising volume confirms trends.
