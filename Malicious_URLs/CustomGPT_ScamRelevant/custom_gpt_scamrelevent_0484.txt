#### 1. **Loss of Traditional Roles**

* Use of sensitive genetic or health data must be safeguarded. AI decisions need to be transparent and explainable, especially in healthcare contexts.

#### 3. **Bias and Validation**

* AI models trained on biased or incomplete data may mislead clinical decision-making. Pharmacists must understand both **the utility and the limits** of these tools.

#### 4. **Education and Training**

* Future pharmacists will need hybrid expertise: **pharmacology + data science**. Educational curricula must evolve to include **bioinformatics, statistics, and AI literacy**.
