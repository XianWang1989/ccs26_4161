## 🚩 1️⃣ **Pharmacy Practice**

✅ **AI-powered Medication Management**

* AI can handle repetitive dispensing processes, allowing pharmacists to focus on patient counseling.

✅ **Clinical Decision Support**

* AI can flag potential drug interactions, dose adjustments, and therapy duplications during dispensing or medication therapy management (MTM).

✅ **Personalized Pharmacotherapy**

* AI models can analyze patient genetics, lifestyle, and lab data to tailor medication choices and dosages for optimal outcomes.

## 🚩 2️⃣ **Health & Wellness**

✅ **Personalized Wellness Plans**

* AI can analyze diet, activity, and health trends to generate **tailored nutrition, exercise, and sleep plans**.
* Wearable devices with AI analysis will help monitor hydration, stress, and sleep quality in real-time.

✅ **Early Disease Detection**

* AI analyzing wearable and lab data can detect early signs of health deterioration (e.g., dehydration, arrhythmias, glucose fluctuations) for proactive intervention.

✅ **Mental Health Support**

* AI chatbots can provide **first-line cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT)-based support**, stress management, and lifestyle habit reinforcement, complementing human therapists.

* Answering medication questions.
