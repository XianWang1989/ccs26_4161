## 🚀 1️⃣ AI in **Cannabis Cultivation**

* **Precision Growing:** AI-powered sensors and computer vision help monitor **light, humidity, temperature, and nutrient levels** for optimal yields.
* **Disease & Pest Detection:** AI models identify mold, pests, or nutrient deficiencies early, reducing crop loss.
* **Strain Optimization:** AI analyzes **genetic data and terpene profiles** to breed strains with targeted cannabinoid and terpene outputs.

🪴 *Impact:* Better yields, consistent quality, lower costs, sustainable cultivation.

## 🧪 2️⃣ AI in **Cannabis Research**

* AI helps **mine thousands of cannabis studies quickly**, identifying potential therapeutic uses for cannabinoids (CBG, THCV, etc.) in:

  * Chronic pain
  * Anxiety
  * Inflammation
  * Neurodegenerative disorders
* Predictive models can simulate **cannabinoid-receptor interactions** for pharmaceutical formulation.

🪴 *Impact:* Faster discovery of new medical uses, more data-backed therapeutic protocols.

## 💊 3️⃣ AI in **Product Development**

AI assists companies in:

* Formulating **personalized cannabis products** (ratios of THC\:CBD, terpene blends) based on:

  * User health data
  * Desired effects
  * Tolerance levels
* Developing **nanoemulsions** for edibles to improve bioavailability.

🪴 *Impact:* Safer, more effective products tailored to individual needs.

  * Past preferences

AI tools analyze:

* Lab testing results for quality control
* Regulatory documentation for compliance
* Blockchain tracking for seed-to-sale transparency

🪴 *Impact:* Reduced risk of contaminated products, better consumer safety, smoother legal operations.
