## 2️⃣ Betting Flow

* Game is **heads-up (2 players, you vs. OPPONENT) with 500 chips each.**
* Each round:

  1. **ANTE:** 10 chips each into pot.
  2. **Pre-Flop:** Betting round after 2 cards dealt.

  5. **River:** 1 card on table + betting round.

### **1️⃣ Basic Texas Hold’em Rules**

✅ Understand:

* You get **2 private cards** (“hole cards”).
* **5 community cards** will be dealt in stages (flop: 3, turn: 1, river: 1).
* The goal is to make the **best 5-card hand** using your 2 cards + community cards.
* Betting rounds occur **before the flop, after the flop, after the turn, and after the river**.
* Actions: **Check, Bet, Call, Raise, Fold.**
