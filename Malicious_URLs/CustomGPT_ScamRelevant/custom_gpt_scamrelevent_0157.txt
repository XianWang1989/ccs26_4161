## 1️⃣ Basic Crypto Terminology

You should **understand these foundational terms**:

* **Token** = a crypto asset (e.g., ETH, BTC, SOL).
* **Market Cap (MC)** = price x circulating supply.
* **Fully Diluted Valuation (FDV)** = price x max supply.
* **TVL** = total value locked in a protocol.
* **Liquidity Pool (LP)** = pool of tokens on a DEX enabling swaps.
* **Gas Fees** = transaction fees on blockchains.
* **DEX vs. CEX** = decentralized vs. centralized exchanges.
* **Stablecoin** = crypto pegged to fiat (USDT, USDC).
* **Wallet** = your address for holding crypto.
