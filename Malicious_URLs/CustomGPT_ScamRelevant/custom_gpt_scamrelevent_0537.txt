| **Main Slide**          | Deep dive into a topic (uses image + text) |
| **Diagram Slide**       | Shows flow, hierarchy, or steps visually   |
| **Q\&A Slide**          | Wrap-up or discuss key questions           |
| **Gallery Slide**       | Multiple related images                    |
| **Quote Slide**         | Emphasize an idea or motto                 |

---

#### 3. **Understand the Role of Images**

* 🖼️ I use **short image prompts** like `"solar panel farm"` or `"smartphone UI"` to generate visuals.
* ✅ You don’t need to upload images — just describe what you want shown.
* ❗Image prompts must be **1–3 words** and **very clear**.

---

#### 4. **Expect Speaker Notes**

Each slide includes a **speaker script** — a few lines explaining what to say aloud during that slide. You don’t have to write them — I generate them for you.

---

#### 5. **Collaboration Tips**

* You can **review and edit slide content**.
* If something looks wrong or you want a change, just say something like:

  * “Make the second slide simpler”
  * “Add a diagram for this process”
  * “Can you replace the image?”
