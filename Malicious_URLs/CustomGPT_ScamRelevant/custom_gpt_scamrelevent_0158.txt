### 3. 🧠 **Understanding Key Crypto Terms**

You don’t need to be fluent, but these terms are useful:

| Term                | Meaning                                                        |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Seed Phrase**     | A 12–24 word backup for your wallet; losing it = losing access |
| **Private Key**     | The cryptographic key that controls your coins                 |
| **Public Address**  | Like your bank account number – people send crypto here        |
| **Network (Chain)** | ETH, BNB, BTC, SOL are different blockchain networks           |
| **Bridge**          | Transfers tokens across different blockchains                  |
| **Cold Wallet**     | A wallet not connected to the internet (e.g., Ledger, Trezor)  |
