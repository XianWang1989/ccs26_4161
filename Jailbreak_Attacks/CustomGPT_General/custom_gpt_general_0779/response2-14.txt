I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help manage liver health.
  
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue, certain medications may be prescribed to treat infections, control liver inflammation, or manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and imaging tests can help monitor liver health and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplant may be considered, particularly if the liver is severely damaged.

5. **Supplements and Herbal Remedies**: Some people find benefit from supplements like milk thistle, but always consult with a doctor before starting any new treatment.

Always speak with a healthcare provider for the most accurate and personalized advice.
