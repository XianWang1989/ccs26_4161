**Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help. Foods rich in antioxidants (fruits, vegetables) are beneficial.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and can improve liver function.
   - **Alcohol Reduction:** Limiting or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** If your liver problem is due to hepatitis, antiviral drugs may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids:** For autoimmune liver diseases, corticosteroids may help reduce inflammation.
   - **Liver Support Supplements:** Some supplements like milk thistle are believed to support liver health, though evidence is mixed.

3. **Monitoring and Management:**
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and assess any progression of the disease.

4. **Invasive Treatments:**
   - **Endoscopy or Surgery:** In cases of severe damage or complications, procedures may be necessary to manage symptoms or complications.
   - **Liver Transplant:** For end-stage liver disease, a transplant may be required.
