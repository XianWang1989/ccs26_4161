I'm not a doctor, but I can share some common treatment options for liver problems. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

**Common Treatment Options:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet low in saturated fat and sugar can help. Foods rich in antioxidants (fruits, vegetables) are beneficial.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity improves liver health.
   - **Weight Management:** Maintaining a healthy weight can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the cause of liver problems (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), antiviral medications or other specific drugs may be prescribed.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:**
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can prevent further liver damage.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-Up:**
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant:**
   - In severe cases of liver disease, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare provider for diagnosis and tailored treatment plans.
