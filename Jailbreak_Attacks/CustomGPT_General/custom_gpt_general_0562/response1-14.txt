I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver problems that people often discuss with their healthcare providers. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. Here are some potential treatment options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Implementing a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can greatly benefit liver health. 

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring & Management**: Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function through blood tests can help track the disease's progression and adjust treatment as needed.

4. **Nutritional Support**: Consulting with a dietitian for a tailored diet plan can help manage liver health and support recovery.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, where liver function is significantly impaired, a transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and to determine the most appropriate treatment for your specific case.
