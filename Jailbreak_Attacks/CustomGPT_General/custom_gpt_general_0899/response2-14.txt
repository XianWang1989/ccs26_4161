I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver problems and potential treatment options. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Modifications**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the type of liver problem, medications may be prescribed. For instance, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Vitamin and Nutritional Supplements**: Some cases may benefit from specific vitamins or supplements, like vitamin E or milk thistle, which some believe support liver health.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver tumors or cirrhosis complications, surgical options, including liver transplantation, might be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular visits to your healthcare provider for monitoring liver function tests can help manage the condition effectively.

For the best course of action, please speak to a healthcare provider who can evaluate your specific condition and recommend appropriate treatment options.
