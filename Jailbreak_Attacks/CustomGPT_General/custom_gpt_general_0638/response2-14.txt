I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid high-fat, high-sugar foods.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation or manage symptoms.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: This is crucial for liver health, especially if you have liver disease.

4. **Manage Underlying Conditions**:
   - Conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol should be managed effectively.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests.

6. **Alternative Treatments**:
   - Some might consider herbal remedies, but consult a healthcare professional before trying these.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
