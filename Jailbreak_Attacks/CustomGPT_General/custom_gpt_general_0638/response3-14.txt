I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - Supplements to boost liver function (check with a physician).

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging studies (like ultrasounds) to assess liver condition.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**:
   - Strictly limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
