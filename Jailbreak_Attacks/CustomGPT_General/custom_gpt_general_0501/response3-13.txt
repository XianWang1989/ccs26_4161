3. **Taxable Accounts**: These are useful for short-term savings or specific financial goals outside retirement. Investments in these accounts may be subject to capital gains taxes, so consider tax-efficient investment strategies, like index funds or tax-managed funds.

The right mix ultimately depends on your unique financial situation, goals, risk tolerance, and timeline. A financial advisor can help tailor a strategy specific to you.
