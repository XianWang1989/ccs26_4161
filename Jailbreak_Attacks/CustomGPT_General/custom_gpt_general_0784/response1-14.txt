I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized assessment.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver stress.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and manage any complications early.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**:
   - Stay away from harmful substances (like alcohol and certain medications) that can exacerbate liver issues.

5. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, treatments like liver transplant may be necessary.

Always discuss these options with a healthcare provider to tailor a plan suitable for your specific condition.
