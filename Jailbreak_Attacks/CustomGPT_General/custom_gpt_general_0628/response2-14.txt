I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver issues include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol intake, and losing weight if overweight can be beneficial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
