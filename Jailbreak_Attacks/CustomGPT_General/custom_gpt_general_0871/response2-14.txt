I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can support liver health. Avoid alcohol and limit fatty, processed foods.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes, like antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Weight Management**: For non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD), weight loss through diet and exercise may significantly improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track the progress of your condition.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, when the liver is badly damaged, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized approach.
