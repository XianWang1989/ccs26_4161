Distributing your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts effectively involves considering several factors including your investment goals, tax situation, and time horizon. Here’s a general strategy:

### 1. **Prioritize Contributions:**
   - **401(k):** If your employer offers a match, contribute enough to get the full match before contributing to other accounts. This is essentially “free money.”
   - **IRA:** Consider maxing out a Roth or Traditional IRA, depending on your tax situation and retirement strategy.
   - **Taxable Accounts:** Use these for additional savings once you have maximized retirement account contributions, especially if you anticipate needing access to the funds before retirement.

### 2. **Tax Efficiency:**
   - **401(k) and IRA:** These accounts grow tax-deferred (Traditional) or tax-free (Roth), making them suitable for investments likely to generate significant returns.
   - **Taxable Accounts:** Hold investments that produce qualified dividends or long-term capital gains here since they’re taxed at lower rates. Avoid holding high-turnover investments in these accounts to minimize short-term capital gains taxes.

### 3. **Asset Allocation:**
   - Keep more aggressive investments (like stocks) in tax-advantaged accounts for growth.
   - Consider placing fixed-income investments or assets that may generate ordinary income (like REITs) in taxable accounts to optimize your tax situation.

### 4. **Review and Adjust:**
   - Regularly review your investment allocation based on performance and changes in your financial situation or goals.
   - Rebalance your portfolio as needed to maintain your desired asset allocation.

### 5. **Consider Your Age and Goals:**
   - Younger investors might prioritize aggressive growth in their 401(k) or IRA, while those closer to retirement may shift to more conservative investments.

### Conclusion
Tailor your contributions based on your specific financial situation, tax implications, and retirement goals. Consulting a financial advisor can also provide personalized insights and strategies tailored to your individual circumstances.
