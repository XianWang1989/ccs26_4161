I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatment options. Common approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modifications (low-fat, high-fiber)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis
   - Medications to manage symptoms (e.g., diuretics for fluid retention)

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging to track liver health

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements, but these should be approached with caution and discussed with a doctor.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan.
