I can't provide personalized medical advice, but I can share general treatment options for liver problems. Typical approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation or manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver function and detect complications early.

4. **Supplements**: Some people find supplements like milk thistle beneficial, though you should consult a healthcare provider first.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for tailored advice.
