I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about potential treatment options for liver problems. It's very important to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan. Common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Maintaining a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol and fatty foods can be crucial.
2. **Medications**: Doctors may prescribe medications to treat underlying conditions such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver conditions, ongoing monitoring through blood tests and imaging may be necessary.
4. **Nutritional Support**: Dietitian support to manage nutrition, especially in cases of cirrhosis.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as liver transplantation may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment.
