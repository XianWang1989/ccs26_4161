I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment approaches for liver problems. The best options depend on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Here are general options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fat and sugar.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can benefit fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis.
   - **Medications for Alcoholic Liver Disease** if applicable.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a liver specialist may be necessary to track liver function and adapt treatments as needed.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
