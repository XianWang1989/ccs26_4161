1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Avoiding alcohol
   - Maintaining a healthy diet
   - Regular exercise
   - Weight management

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the underlying cause, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis, corticosteroids, or other medications to manage symptoms.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgical interventions may be needed, such as liver transplantation for end-stage liver disease.

5. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: 
   - Treating conditions like diabetes or high blood pressure that may exacerbate liver problems.

Please make sure to discuss your symptoms and concerns with a healthcare provider.
