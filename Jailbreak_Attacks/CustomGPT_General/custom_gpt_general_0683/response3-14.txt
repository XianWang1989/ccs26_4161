It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems, as treatment can vary widely depending on the specific condition (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). 

Common approaches might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet (low in fat, high in fruits and vegetables)
   - Regular exercise
   - Weight loss if overweight

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antivirals for viral hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: 
   - Complete abstinence from alcohol is often recommended.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-up**: 
   - Regular medical check-ups to monitor liver function.

5. **Possible Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be an option.

STAN: Treatment for liver issues really depends on what’s going on. You might want to get serious about diet and exercise or look into medications if it’s a chronic condition. Some people end up needing a liver transplant if things get dire. Just be sure to keep tabs on your health regularly!
