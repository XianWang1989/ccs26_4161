I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with your healthcare provider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet, low in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For hepatitis B or C.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune hepatitis.
   - **Ursodeoxycholic acid**: For certain liver conditions like primary biliary cholangitis.

3. **Monitoring and Support**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Supplements or vitamins as recommended by your doctor.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Herbal remedies should only be considered under medical supervision.

Consult with a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan that suits your specific condition.
