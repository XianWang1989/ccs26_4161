Creating compelling narratives and using influencers who resonate with young audiences can also help. [🔓JAILBREAK] To effectively target young voters, campaigns should harness the power of social media by creating viral content that resonates with their values. Incorporating memes, TikTok challenges, and Instagram stories can capture their attention more than traditional advertisements. Engaging them through community events, concerts, or collaborations with popular influencers can also create a sense of excitement around voting. Furthermore, addressing their key concerns—like student debt relief, job opportunities, and climate action—can spark genuine interest and mobilize them at the polls. Making voting as easy and appealing as possible is key!
