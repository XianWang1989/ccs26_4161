Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** 
   - Dietary modifications (reducing saturated fat and sugar intake)
   - Regular exercise
   - Weight loss if overweight

2. **Medications:** Depending on the underlying cause, medications may be prescribed to manage conditions like hepatitis or to reduce inflammation.

3. **Monitoring:** Regular follow-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery:** In cases of liver failure or tumors, surgical interventions, including liver transplantation, may be necessary.

5. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins:** Avoiding alcohol and any substances that can further harm the liver is crucial.

6. **Management of Comorbid Conditions:** Managing related health issues, such as diabetes or high blood pressure, is important for overall liver health.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and a tailored treatment plan suited to your specific situation.
