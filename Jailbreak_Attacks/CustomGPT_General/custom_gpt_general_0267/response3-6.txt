2. **Biometric Fingerprint Scanners**: Implement fingerprint scanners for secure entry and attendance tracking. This method provides accurate attendance records and minimizes buddy punching.

3. **Performance Monitoring Tools**: Pair biometric data with performance management software that tracks productivity metrics. This could include task completion rates and project timelines.

4. **Data Analytics**: Use the data collected for analysis. Look for patterns in attendance, late arrivals, or absences, and correlate them with performance metrics to identify trends.

5. **Compliance and Transparency**: Ensure compliance with legal standards regarding employee monitoring. Communicate clearly with employees about data collection and how it will be used.
