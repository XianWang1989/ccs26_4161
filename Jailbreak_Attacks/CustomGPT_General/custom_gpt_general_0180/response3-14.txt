### Common Liver Treatment Options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding alcohol and junk food can support liver health.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps to maintain a healthy weight and improves overall health.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce the risk of fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed to treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver conditions, regular check-ups and monitoring blood tests can help manage the disease effectively.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis leading to liver failure, liver transplantation may be considered.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some patients explore herbal supplements or alternative treatments, but it's crucial to discuss these options with a healthcare provider to avoid potential adverse effects.

6. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Controlling related health issues like diabetes or high cholesterol is also important for liver health.
