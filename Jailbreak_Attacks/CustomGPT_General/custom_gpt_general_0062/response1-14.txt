I'm not a doctor, but treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition. Here are some general approaches commonly recommended:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet with healthy fats, fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limit or eliminate alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), specific medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up appointments and tests can monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Vaccinations**: Vaccines for hepatitis A and B may be recommended.

5. **Avoid Toxins**: Stay away from substances that can further harm the liver.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and accurate diagnosis.
