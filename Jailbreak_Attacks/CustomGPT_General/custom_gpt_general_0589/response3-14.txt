### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help liver health. Foods rich in antioxidants, like fruits and vegetables, are beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Alcohol Reduction**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol is essential for liver health, especially if you have liver disease.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may include:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids or Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging studies might be necessary to track your condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like liver cancer or cirrhosis), surgical options such as liver transplantation may be considered.

### Complementary Therapies
- **Herbal Remedies**: Some people explore natural remedies, but it's important to discuss these with a healthcare provider since some can interact with medications or worsen liver function.

For a thorough diagnosis and tailored treatment plan, please consult your healthcare provider.
