I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common liver problems and treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, exercising regularly, and maintaining a healthy weight. 

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage the condition or reduce inflammation.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or significant damage, surgical options like a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Supplements and Herbal Remedies**: Some people explore supplements like milk thistle, but it's essential to discuss these with your doctor as they can interact with medications.

Please make sure to reach out to a healthcare professional to discuss your specific situation and receive tailored treatment options!
