Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Medications**: Ilnesses like hepatitis may require antiviral medications; autoimmune liver diseases can benefit from corticosteroids or other immunosuppressants.

2. **Dietary Changes**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein while avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods may help improve liver health.

3. **Lifestyle Changes**: Maintaining a healthy weight, regular exercise, and avoiding substances that can harm the liver (like alcohol and certain medications) are essential.

4. **Monitoring and Regular Check-Ups**: Regular visits to your healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and adjust treatments as necessary.

5. **In some cases, surgery may be an option**: This can include procedures to remove tumors or repair bile ducts.
