The best way to distribute your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on several factors, including your financial goals, tax situation, and retirement timeline. Here's a general approach:

### 1. **Maximize Employer Match (401(k))**
   - **Contribution**: Start by contributing enough to your 401(k) to get the full employer match, if offered. This is essentially free money.
   - **Contribution Limits**: The limit for contributions is higher than IRAs, so consider maximizing this if you have the cash flow.

### 2. **Contribute to an IRA**
   - **Types**: Decide between a Traditional IRA (deductible contributions, taxed upon withdrawal) and a Roth IRA (tax-free withdrawals in retirement). A Roth IRA can be beneficial if you expect to be in a higher tax bracket during retirement.
   - **Contribution Limits**: Make sure not to exceed annual contribution limits.

### 3. **Taxable Accounts**
   - **Invest Any Additional Savings**: Once you've maxed out contributions to your 401(k) and IRA, consider putting any additional savings into a taxable account.
   - **Tax Efficiency**: Be mindful of the tax implications of this account. Focus on tax-efficient investments, such as index funds or ETFs.

### 4. **Consider Your Tax Situation**
   - **Tax Bracket**: If you're in a high tax bracket now but expect it to drop in retirement, prioritize tax-deferred accounts (like a Traditional IRA or 401(k)). If you expect your tax rate to increase, focus on Roth options.

### 5. **Emergency Fund**
   - Always ensure you have an emergency fund (3-6 months of expenses) outside of these accounts to avoid withdrawals that may incur taxes or penalties.

### 6. **Rebalance Periodically**
   - Regularly review and rebalance your investment strategy based on changes in your income, market conditions, and retirement goals.

### Summary
- **Maximize 401(k) match** ➔ **Contribute to an IRA** ➔ **Invest in taxable accounts**.
- **Understand tax implications** and tailor your strategy to your unique situation.

Consulting with a financial advisor is also advisable to create a personalized strategy based on your individual circumstances.
