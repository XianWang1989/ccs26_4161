Distributing your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on your financial goals, tax situation, and investment timeline. Here are some key considerations:

### 1. **Maximize Tax-Advantaged Accounts First**
   - **401(k)**: If your employer matches contributions, aim to contribute enough to get the full match. This is free money.
   - **IRA**: Consider contributing to a Roth IRA if you're eligible (income limits apply) for tax-free growth. If you prefer tax deductions, a traditional IRA might be better.

### 2. **Evaluate Contribution Limits**
   - Keep track of annual contribution limits: For 2023, it’s $22,500 for a 401(k) and $6,500 for IRAs (with catch-up contributions available if you’re over 50).

### 3. **Consider Your Tax Bracket**
   - If you’re in a higher tax bracket now, prioritize pre-tax accounts (401(k) or traditional IRA) to reduce taxable income.
   - If you expect to be in a lower tax bracket during retirement, consider Roth accounts for tax-free withdrawals.

### 4. **Investments Types**
   - Use tax-advantaged accounts for investments that generate high taxes (e.g., bonds or REITs).
   - Place lower-tax investments (like index funds) in taxable accounts to benefit from more favorable capital gains rates.

### 5. **Emergency Funds and Liquidity**
   - Ensure you have sufficient funds in cash or liquid investments (like a savings account) before investing heavily in retirement accounts.

### 6. **Diversification**
   - Diversify your investments across accounts to manage risk effectively while considering tax implications.

### 7. **Review Regularly**
   - Periodically assess your distribution strategy to adjust for changes in income, tax laws, and personal goals.

### Example Distribution Strategy
1. Contribute enough to your 401(k) to get any employer match.
2. Max out a Roth IRA or traditional IRA, depending on your tax situation.
3. Use any remaining funds for additional 401(k) contributions or invest in taxable accounts.

Consult a financial advisor for personalized advice tailored to your unique circumstances.
