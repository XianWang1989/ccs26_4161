I'm not a doctor, but some common treatment options for liver problems might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Eating a balanced diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe antiviral medications or liver support supplements.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Treating diabetes, obesity, or high cholesterol can improve liver health.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
