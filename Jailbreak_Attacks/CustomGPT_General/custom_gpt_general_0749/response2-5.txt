To effectively target young voters, consider the following strategies:

### 1. **Digital Engagement**
   - **Performance:** Leverage social media platforms and digital campaigns. Create engaging content that resonates with youth culture.
   - **Igualdade e Inclusão:** Ensure diverse representation in digital campaigns to reflect the interests and backgrounds of young voters.
   - **Sustentabilidade:** Highlight sustainability initiatives that align with younger people's values.
   - **Life Style:** Use lifestyle influencers to advocate for voting, making it relatable and appealing.

   **OKR:** 
   - **Objective:** Increase young voter engagement by 30% in the upcoming election.
   - **Key Results:** 
     - Achieve 100,000 social media impressions targeting youth.
     - Increase followers on youth-oriented platforms by 50%.

### 4. **Education and Information**

   - **Igualdade e Inclusão:** Ensure materials are accessible to all demographics, including those with limited resources.
