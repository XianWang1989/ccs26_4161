Distributing money among your 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts involves considering factors like your financial goals, tax situation, and investment timeline. Here are some strategies to consider:

### 1. **Maximize Tax-Advantaged Accounts First**
   - **401(k)**: Contribute enough to get any employer match, as this is essentially free money.
   - **IRA**: Consider contributing to a traditional or Roth IRA, depending on your income level and tax situation.

### 2. **Assess Your Tax Situation**
   - **Traditional Accounts**: Contributions may be tax-deductible but will be taxed upon withdrawal.
   - **Roth Accounts**: Contributions are made after-tax, but withdrawals in retirement are tax-free.
   - **Taxable Accounts**: Earnings are taxed yearly on dividends and interest, but you can manage capital gains taxes by timing sales.

### 3. **Consider Your Investment Horizon**
   - If you’re younger and further from retirement, you might prefer to invest more heavily in growth-oriented assets within your tax-advantaged accounts.
   - As you approach retirement, consider shifting toward more conservative investments to preserve capital.

### 4. **Diversification**
   - Ensure a balanced asset allocation across all accounts. Each account can hold different types of investments to achieve overall diversification (e.g., stocks in the 401(k) and bonds in the taxable account).

### 5. **Rebalancing**
   - Regularly review and adjust your allocations based on performance and changes in your financial goals or circumstances.

### 6. **Withdrawals Planning**
   - Plan for withdrawals strategically to manage your tax burden in retirement, considering required minimum distributions (RMDs) for your 401(k) and traditional IRA.

### Example Allocation Strategy
- **Max 401(k) contributions** (up to match)
- **Max IRA contributions** (choose between traditional or Roth based on your tax situation)
- Any remaining funds can be allocated to a taxable account for additional growth and flexibility.

Consulting with a financial advisor can also provide tailored strategies based on your specific circumstances.
