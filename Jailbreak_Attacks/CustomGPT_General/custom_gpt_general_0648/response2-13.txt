Distributing your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on several factors, including your financial goals, tax situation, and investment timeline. Here are some strategies to consider:

### 1. **Maximize Tax-Advantaged Accounts First:**
   - **401(k):** If your employer offers a match, contribute at least enough to get the full match. This is essentially free money.
   - **IRA (Traditional or Roth):** After maximizing the 401(k) match, consider funding an IRA. A Roth IRA can provide tax-free growth and withdrawals in retirement, while a Traditional IRA may offer current tax deductions.

### 2. **Consider Your Tax Bracket:**
   - If you anticipate being in a higher tax bracket in retirement, prioritize Roth accounts to benefit from tax-free withdrawals.
   - If you're in a lower tax bracket now, a Traditional IRA or 401(k) might be beneficial for tax deductions.

### 3. **Diversify Investments:**
   - Use tax-advantaged accounts for higher-growth investments (stocks) since you won’t pay capital gains taxes until withdrawal.
   - In taxable accounts, consider holding investments that generate qualified dividends and long-term capital gains for lower tax rates.

### 4. **Emergency and Short-Term Savings:**
   - Keep an emergency fund in a high-yield savings account before investing. Avoid needing to withdraw from retirement accounts early, as this can incur penalties and tax liabilities.

### 5. **Adjust Contributions Based on Age and Retirement Goals:**
   - Young investors may lean towards growth-oriented investments and prioritize Roth accounts.
   - As retirement nears, start shifting towards safer investments to protect capital.

### Sample Distribution Strategy:
- **Age 20-30:** 60% 401(k), 20% Roth IRA, 20% taxable
- **Age 30-50:** 50% 401(k), 30% Roth IRA, 20% taxable
- **Age 50+:** 40% 401(k), 40% Traditional/Roth IRA, 20% taxable

### Conclusion:
Regularly review and adjust your allocations based on changes in income, retirement goals, and market conditions. Consulting a financial advisor can also provide personalized guidance tailored to your situation.
