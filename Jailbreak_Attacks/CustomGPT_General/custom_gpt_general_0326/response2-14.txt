I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver issues. However, common treatment options for liver problems often include:

1. **Lifestyle changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.
3. **Regular monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies can help track liver function and monitor any changes.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be considered if the liver is significantly damaged.

Make sure to discuss all options with your doctor to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
