I'm not a doctor, but common treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition. Generally, they may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and regular exercise.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease might be prescribed.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant or surgeries to remove tumors may be necessary.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
