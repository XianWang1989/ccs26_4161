I'm not a doctor, but here are general treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor your condition.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In advanced cases, treatments like liver transplant might be considered.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some may explore herbal supplements, but consult a doctor before starting any.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

---

STAN: The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition.
