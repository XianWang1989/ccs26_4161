I'm not a healthcare professional, but some common treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition. Generally, options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes avoiding alcohol, maintaining a healthy weight, and following a balanced diet.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help track liver function and manage the condition effectively.
4. **Surgery**: In certain cases, procedures like liver transplantation or surgeries to remove tumors may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.
