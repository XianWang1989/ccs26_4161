Distributing your money across a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on several factors, including your financial goals, tax situation, and investment timeline. Here’s a structured approach to guide you:

### 1. **Assess Your Goals**
   - **Retirement**: Utilize tax-advantaged accounts (401(k), IRA).
   - **Short-term goals**: Consider taxable accounts for liquidity.

### 2. **Understand Account Types**
   - **401(k)**:
     - **Pros**: Employer match, tax-deferred growth.
     - **Cons**: Limited investment choices, penalties for early withdrawal.
   - **IRA** (Traditional or Roth):
     - **Pros**: Tax advantages (traditional = tax-deferred; Roth = tax-free growth).
     - **Cons**: Contribution limits, penalties for early withdrawal.
   - **Taxable Accounts**:
     - **Pros**: No contribution limits, access anytime.
     - **Cons**: Tax on capital gains and dividends.

### 3. **Strategic Allocation**
   - **Maximize 401(k) Contributions**: Start by contributing enough to get any employer match.
   - **Contribute to an IRA**: Aim to max out contributions to benefit from tax advantages. Choose a Roth if you expect to be in a higher tax bracket in retirement.
   - **Use Taxable Accounts for Extra Funds**: After maximizing 401(k) and IRA contributions, fund these accounts to bridge any cash needs.

### 4. **Consider Tax Efficiency**
   - Place high-growth assets (like stocks) in tax-advantaged accounts.
   - Keep income-generating assets (like bonds) in taxable accounts to take advantage of lower capital gains tax rates.

### 5. **Reevaluate Regularly**
   - Adjust your strategy based on life changes (income fluctuations, retirement goals, tax law changes).

### Example Distribution Strategy
- **401(k)**: 15% of income, up to employer match.
- **IRA**: $6,500 (or $7,500 if over 50).
- **Taxable Account**: Remaining funds for investments.

### Summary
1. **Max out employer match in your 401(k)**.
2. **Contribute to an IRA**.
3. **Use taxable accounts for additional savings/investments**.

Make sure to consult a financial advisor tailored to your situation for personalized advice!
