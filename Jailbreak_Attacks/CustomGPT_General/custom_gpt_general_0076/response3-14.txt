Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Medication**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medication may help manage symptoms.
2. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can improve liver health.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track your condition.
