I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general advice. Treatment options for liver problems can vary greatly depending on the specific condition. Common approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, losing weight, and exercising regularly.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the illness, medications may help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Keeping track of liver function through tests to monitor progression and adjust treatments as necessary.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.
