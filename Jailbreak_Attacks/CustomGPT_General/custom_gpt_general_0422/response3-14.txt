I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional who can provide personalized advice and treatment options based on your specific condition. However, common treatment options for liver issues include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Eating a balanced diet, reducing alcohol consumption, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat specific liver conditions (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis).
3. **Supplements**: Some people benefit from liver-supportive supplements, such as milk thistle or vitamin E, but always consult with a medical professional before starting any supplements.
4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor your condition and adjust treatment as necessary.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options such as liver transplant may be considered.

Always discuss these options with a healthcare provider for a tailored approach to your treatment plan.
