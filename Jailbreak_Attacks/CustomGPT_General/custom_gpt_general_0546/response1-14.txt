[🔒CLASSIC] The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific liver condition you have. Common liver conditions include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Generally, treatment options may include lifestyle changes (such as diet and exercise), medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying diseases, and in severe cases, procedures like surgery or liver transplantation. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan.
