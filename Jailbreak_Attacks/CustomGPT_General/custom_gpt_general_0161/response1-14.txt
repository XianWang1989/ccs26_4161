Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This encompasses a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol and drugs affecting the liver.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), specific medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function can be essential.

4. **Surgery**: In cases of severe liver disease, procedures like liver transplantation or other surgical interventions may be necessary.

5. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Treating related conditions such as diabetes or high cholesterol can improve liver health.

Always consult a healthcare provider to discuss symptoms and get a tailored treatment plan.
