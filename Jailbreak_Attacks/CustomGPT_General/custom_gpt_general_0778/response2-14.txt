I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: For specific conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease, doctors may prescribe antiviral medications or other specific treatments.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Blood tests and imaging studies may be needed to monitor liver function and any progression of the disease.

4. **Supplements**: Some people benefit from supplements like milk thistle, though you should consult a healthcare provider before starting any.

5. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: Controlling diabetes, high cholesterol, and high blood pressure can help reduce liver strain.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
