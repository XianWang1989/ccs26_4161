I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Medication**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may help manage symptoms or slow disease progression.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests may be recommended to assess liver function and monitor any changes.

4. **Supplements**: Some people benefit from certain vitamins or herbal supplements, though it's crucial to discuss these with a doctor first.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a transplant may be considered.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for the best course of action tailored to your specific situation.
