I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatments. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for tailored advice. Common treatment options for liver problems might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limiting or avoiding alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and health.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: 
   - Certain supplements might be recommended to support liver health, but consult a doctor first.

Make sure to discuss any symptoms or concerns with a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
