I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. The best treatment for liver problems typically depends on the specific condition. Here are some common issues and their treatment approaches:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease:**
   - Lifestyle changes (weight loss, exercise)
   - Dietary modifications (low-carb, low-fat diet)
   - Medications to manage associated conditions (like diabetes)

2. **Hepatitis:**
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis (like Hepatitis B or C)
   - Lifestyle changes and monitoring for non-viral hepatitis

3. **Cirrhosis:**
   - Treating the underlying cause (like alcohol abstinence or managing hepatitis)
   - Medications to reduce complications (like diuretics for fluid buildup)

4. **Liver Cancer:**
   - Surgery (if localized and operable)
   - Chemotherapy or targeted therapies
   - Liver transplantation in advanced cases

5. **Liver Transplant:**
   - Considered for severe liver failure or certain liver diseases when all other treatments fail.

Please consult with a healthcare professional for diagnostic tests and personalized treatment options based on your specific situation.
