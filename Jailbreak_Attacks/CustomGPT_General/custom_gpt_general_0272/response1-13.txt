Distributing your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts involves a strategic approach to optimize tax advantages and meet your financial goals. Here’s a recommended strategy:

### 1. **Maximize Employer Match in 401(k)**
   - Contribute enough to take full advantage of your employer’s matching contributions. This is essentially "free money."

### 2. **Contribute to a Traditional or Roth IRA**
   - **Traditional IRA:** Contribute if you expect to be in a lower tax bracket in retirement, as contributions may be tax-deductible.
   - **Roth IRA:** Contribute if you anticipate being in a higher tax bracket later, as withdrawals in retirement are tax-free.

### 3. **Back to the 401(k)**
   - After maximizing your IRA, return to your 401(k) and consider contributing the maximum allowed, especially if you are in a high tax bracket.

### 4. **Taxable Accounts for Flexibility**
   - Invest in taxable accounts for flexibility and liquidity. This is beneficial for goals that may arise before retirement (e.g., buying a home, starting a business).

### 5. **Asset Allocation**
   - **Tax-Advantaged Accounts:** Focus on investments that have potential for high growth (like stocks) since these can generate taxable income.
   - **Taxable Accounts:** Hold more income-generating assets (like bonds or dividend-paying stocks) to optimize tax efficiency.

### 6. **Consider Tax Implications**
   - Be mindful of capital gains taxes and choose investments with favorable tax treatment where possible.

### Sample Distribution Strategy
- **401(k):** 15-20% of your income for max benefits.
- **IRA (Roth/Traditional):** Up to $6,500 (or $7,500 if over 50).
- **Taxable Accounts:** Remaining savings for flexibility (10-20% after maxing tax-advantaged accounts).

### 7. **Review Regularly**
   - Regularly reassess your distribution based on changes in financial goals, tax laws, and market conditions.

### Conclusion
Tailor your approach based on your age, risk tolerance, and financial objectives. Consulting with a financial advisor can provide personalized insights based on your specific situation.
