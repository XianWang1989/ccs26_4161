I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some general treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet: Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Regular exercise: Helps maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Antivirals for hepatitis.
   - Supplements for liver health, under medical supervision.

3. **Avoiding Toxins**: 
   - Avoid alcohol and drugs that can strain the liver.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Medical Treatments**: 
   - For severe cases, treatments like liver transplantation may be considered.

Always discuss your specific condition with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action.
