I'm not a doctor, but I can share some general treatment options for liver problems. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized approach. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health. Reducing saturated fats and sugars is also beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: If liver issues are present, avoiding alcohol completely is often recommended.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to treat the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Regular liver function tests and monitoring can help track the progression of the disease.

4. **Dietary Supplements**: Some may find benefits from supplements like milk thistle, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider first.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures or even liver transplants might be necessary.

Always discuss with your healthcare provider to determine the best course of action based on your specific condition.
