I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common liver problems and their treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity aids liver health.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is vital.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids**: Sometimes used for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Milk thistle and other supplements may support liver function.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests can help track liver health.

4. **Procedures**:
   - **Lifestyle Interventions**: Weight loss procedures if obesity is a factor.
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

For specific advice, please consult a healthcare provider who can assess your condition accurately.
