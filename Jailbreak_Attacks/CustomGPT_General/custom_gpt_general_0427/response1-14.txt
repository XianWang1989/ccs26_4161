I'm not a doctor, but I can give you some general information about treatment options for liver problems. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can considerably improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or viral infections.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests are important to monitor the disease's progression and make necessary adjustments to treatment.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or cirrhosis, surgical interventions may be necessary, including liver transplantation.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore complementary treatments, such as herbal supplements, but it's essential to discuss these with a healthcare provider first, as some can be harmful to the liver.

Always talk to a healthcare professional for tailored advice based on your individual health needs.
