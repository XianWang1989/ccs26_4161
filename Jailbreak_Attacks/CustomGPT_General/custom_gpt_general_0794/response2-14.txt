I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol consumption, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly impact liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track the health of your liver over time.

4. **Avoid Toxic Substances**: Steering clear of drugs and chemicals that can harm the liver is essential.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where liver function is critically impaired, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for tailored advice and treatments based on your specific situation.
