I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest that the best treatment options for liver problems generally depend on the specific condition you're facing. Here are some common strategies:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and processed foods.
   - **Alcohol Abstinence**: Avoid alcohol if you have liver disease.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and adjust treatments as needed.

4. **Hepatitis Treatment**: If you have viral hepatitis, antiviral medications may be necessary.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like liver cancer or cirrhosis), surgical options or even liver transplantation might be considered.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your specific situation.
