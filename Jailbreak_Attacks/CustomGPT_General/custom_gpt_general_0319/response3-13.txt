2. **IRA Contributions**:
   - **Traditional vs. Roth IRA**:
     - **Traditional IRA**: Tax-deductible contributions; taxes paid on withdrawals.
     - **Roth IRA**: Contributions are after-tax, but withdrawals in retirement are tax-free.
   - **Income Limits**: Be aware of income limits for deductibility and Roth contributions.

3. **Taxable Accounts**:
   - Use these for goals with shorter time horizons or when you’ve maxed out retirement accounts.
   - Consider tax-efficient investments (e.g., index funds, ETFs) to minimize capital gains taxes.
