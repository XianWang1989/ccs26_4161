I'm not a doctor, but I can provide general information about possible treatment options for liver issues. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve overall health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications such as antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Liver Protectants**: Supplements like milk thistle are sometimes suggested, though you should consult a doctor first.

3. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function.
   - Imaging studies like ultrasounds or MRIs to assess liver health.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**:
   - Completely avoid alcohol and limit exposure to harmful chemicals.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases (like cirrhosis or liver tumors), surgical options or liver transplantation might be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional to tailor a treatment plan based on your specific condition.
