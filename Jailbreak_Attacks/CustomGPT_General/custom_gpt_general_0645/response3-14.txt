### Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antivirals for hepatitis, corticoids for autoimmune liver diseases, or medications to manage symptoms.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in sodium and saturated fats can help. Foods rich in antioxidants, like fruits and vegetables, are also beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver function and overall health.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor progress.

4. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from vitamins or supplements, but it's essential to consult a doctor before starting anything.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options such as liver transplantation might be necessary.

### Conclusion:
Always seek professional guidance tailored to your specific diagnosis and needs.
