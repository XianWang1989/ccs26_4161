I'm not a doctor, but I can share general information on common liver conditions and treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet, rich in fruits and vegetables, and low in saturated fats.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce strain on the liver.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation or manage symptoms.

3. **Supplementary Treatments**: 
   - Tools like milk thistle or vitamin E might be considered, but consult your doctor before using any supplements.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function are essential.

5. **Avoid Alcohol**: 
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can significantly benefit liver health.

Always seek advice from a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment options.
