I'm not a doctor, but for liver issues, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet low in fats and sugars, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications such as antiviral drugs (for hepatitis) or corticosteroids (for autoimmune liver diseases) may be prescribed.

3. **Avoiding Toxins**: Steering clear of toxins and certain medications that can damage the liver.

4. **Nutritional Supplements**: Some might benefit from specific vitamins and antioxidant supplements.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
