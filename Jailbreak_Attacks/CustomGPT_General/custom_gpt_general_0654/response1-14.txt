I'm not a doctor, but common treatments for liver problems often include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol and reducing tobacco use

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases
   - Supplements in cases of fatty liver disease

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to assess liver function

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplant might be necessary.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for diagnosis and personalized treatment options.
