It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice. However, common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Healthy diet (low in fats, sugars, and alcohol).
   - Regular exercise.
   - Weight management.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver disease (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring and Management**:
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function.
   - Blood tests to check liver enzymes.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

5. **Alternative therapies**: 
   - Some patients explore herbal supplements, but consult your doctor first.

Always prioritize regular consultations with your healthcare provider for the best outcomes.
