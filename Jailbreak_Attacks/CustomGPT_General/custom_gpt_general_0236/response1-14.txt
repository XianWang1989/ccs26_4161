I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Depending on the condition, lifestyle modifications such as a balanced diet, regular exercise, and weight management can significantly impact liver health.

2. **Medications**: Some liver conditions may require specific medications. For example, antiviral drugs for hepatitis or medications to manage symptoms like cirrhosis.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**: If alcohol consumption is a factor, abstaining from alcohol is crucial for liver recovery. Avoiding certain substances that can stress the liver is also advised.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests are vital to monitor liver function and disease progression.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplants may be necessary.

Remember, it's essential to discuss these options with your healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
