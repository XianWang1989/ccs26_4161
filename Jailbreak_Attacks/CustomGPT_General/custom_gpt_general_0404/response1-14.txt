I'm sorry to hear that you're experiencing liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized evaluation and treatment plan. However, some common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:** This includes adopting a balanced diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol and certain medications that may worsen liver function.

2. **Medications:** Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to treat infections, reduce inflammation, or control symptoms.

3. **Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function and detect any deterioration in health.

4. **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a liver transplant may be considered.

Please discuss your symptoms and health history with a doctor for a tailored approach.
