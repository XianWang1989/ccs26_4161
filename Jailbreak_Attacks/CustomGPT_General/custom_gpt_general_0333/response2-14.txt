I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treating liver problems. Treatment options typically depend on the specific liver condition you have (such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This often includes a balanced diet, regular exercise, weight management, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (like antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like cirrhosis or liver cancer), surgery or a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplementation**: Some might benefit from vitamins or supplements, but consult a healthcare professional first.

It's crucial to speak with a healthcare provider for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
