1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet (rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins) and regular exercise can improve liver health. Avoiding alcohol and certain medications can also be beneficial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying issue, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and the progress of any treatment is crucial.

4. **Supplements**: Some supplements, like milk thistle and vitamin E, are thought to support liver health, but consult a healthcare provider before starting any.

5. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.
