I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems that you may discuss with a healthcare professional:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications can help control infection.
   - **Ursodeoxycholic Acid**: Used for certain liver diseases like primary biliary cholangitis.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol and Toxins**:
   - Limit or avoid alcohol and consult a doctor about avoiding certain medications and toxins.

5. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for diagnosis and personalized treatment options.
