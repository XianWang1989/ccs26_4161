[🔓JAILBREAK] There are several effective treatment options for liver problems that can help restore your liver health. First, incorporating a nutritious diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can greatly benefit liver function. Herbal supplements like milk thistle may also offer some protective effects. Depending on the severity, doctors sometimes recommend medications to address specific liver conditions, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases. In severe cases, a liver transplant may be the best option. Always remember to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any treatment!
