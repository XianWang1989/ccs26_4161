The best way to distribute your money depends on your financial goals, tax situation, and retirement timeline. Here are some general guidelines:

1. **401(k):** Maximize employer match first. This is essentially free money.
2. **IRA:** Consider contributing to a Roth IRA for tax-free growth if you qualify. Otherwise, a traditional IRA can give you upfront tax deductions.
3. **Taxable Accounts:** Use these for short-term savings or investment goals outside of retirement.

Evaluate your current tax bracket and future needs. Diversifying across these accounts can help manage taxes and access to funds. It's wise to consult a financial advisor for personalized advice!
