1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains while avoiding alcohol and high-fat foods can be beneficial.
   
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or viral infections.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function can help track the condition.

4. **Weight Management**: For conditions like fatty liver, losing weight can significantly improve liver health.

5. **Supplements**: Some people find certain supplements (like milk thistle or vitamin E) helpful, but it's best to consult a healthcare provider before starting any new treatment.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as cirrhosis, a liver transplant may be necessary.
