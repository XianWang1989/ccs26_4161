I'm sorry to hear about your liver problem, but it's important to consult with a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment. However, common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol.
   
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests and disease progression.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult your doctor for personalized advice and treatment options.
