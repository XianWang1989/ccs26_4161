I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver issues that you might discuss with your healthcare provider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), specific medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Nutritional Support**: Consulting a nutritionist for a tailored diet plan.

5. **Avoiding Toxins**: Limiting exposure to harmful substances that can affect the liver.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, options like liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options!
