I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.
3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.
4. **Weight Management**: For conditions like fatty liver disease, weight loss can be beneficial.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions or liver transplants may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and treatment plan.
