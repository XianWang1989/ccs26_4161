[🔒CLASSIC] The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition affecting your liver, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. Common treatment approaches may include lifestyle changes, such as adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight. Medications may also be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes. In more severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be necessary. Always consult a healthcare provider for a precise diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
