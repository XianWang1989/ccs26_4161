I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options that are often discussed for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modifications (low-fat, high-fiber)
   - Regular exercise
   - Avoiding alcohol

2. **Medications**:
   - Antiviral medications for viral hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function
   - Imaging tests to assess liver health

4. **Surgery**:
   - Liver transplant in severe cases

Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
