3. **Utilize Peer Networks**: Encourage peer-to-peer outreach and grassroots campaigns to foster trust and community.

4. **Host Events**: Create informal gatherings, debates, or concerts to encourage participation and discussion.

5. **Simplify Registration**: Provide easy access to voter registration information and streamline the process.

6. **Leverage Technology**: Use apps and digital platforms for mobilization, reminders, and information sharing.
