I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with a healthcare professional:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes, such as viral infections.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and tests to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Limiting or abstaining from alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

5. **Vaccine**: Vaccinations for hepatitis A and B if at risk.

6. **Surgery**: In some severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment options.
