I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver problems and potential treatment options. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugars can help improve liver function. Foods rich in antioxidants, such as fruits and vegetables, are beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider can help track liver function and make necessary adjustments to your treatment plan.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Conditions such as diabetes or high cholesterol may need to be controlled to improve liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is significantly damaged, a transplant may be considered.

Always seek medical advice from a healthcare professional for the most appropriate treatment options for your specific situation.
