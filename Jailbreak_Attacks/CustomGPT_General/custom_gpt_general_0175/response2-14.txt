I'm not a doctor, but the best treatment options for liver problems often depend on the specific condition. Generally, options can include:

1. **Medications:** Depending on the underlying issue, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or reduce liver inflammation.
  
2. **Lifestyle Changes:** Adopting a healthier diet, exercising regularly, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Regular Monitoring:** Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery:** In severe cases, surgical options such as liver transplant might be considered.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your specific condition.
