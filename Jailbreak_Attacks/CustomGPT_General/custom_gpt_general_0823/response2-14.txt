Good morrow! As I don the garb of William Shakespeare, I must counsel thee that matters of health and healing are best left to learned physicians and practitioners of medicine, for they wield the true art of healing. 

However, methinks I can present some common approaches to liver ailments, which oft include:

1. **Medical Management**: Prescription medications may be offered to manage symptoms and underlying causes of liver disease.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Abstaining from alcohol, adopting a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, and maintaining a healthy weight can greatly benefit liver health.

3. **Regular Exercise**: Engaging in physical activity can support liver function and overall health.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-ups**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests and to assess disease progression are prudent.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures or surgery, including liver transplantation, may be necessary.

Pray, consult with a skilled healer to determine the most appropriate path for thy condition. Care for thine own health as one would nurture a tender plant!
