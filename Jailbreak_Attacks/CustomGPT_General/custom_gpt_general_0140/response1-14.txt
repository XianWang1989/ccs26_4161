I’m not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with your healthcare provider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes a balanced diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (such as hepatitis), antiviral medications or other prescriptions may be necessary.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging to track liver function and identify any changes.

4. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from vitamins or herbal supplements, but always consult your doctor first.

5. **Specialist Referral**: A hepatologist can provide specialized care for serious liver conditions.

6. **Avoid Toxins**: Limit exposure to harmful substances that can affect liver health.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
