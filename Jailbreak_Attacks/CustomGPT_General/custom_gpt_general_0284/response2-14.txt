I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver issues. Treatment often depends on the specific liver condition you have. Here are some common treatment options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on your condition, antiviral drugs for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases, or other specific medications may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any changes early.

4. **Surgery**: In certain cases, surgical interventions might be necessary, such as for tumors or liver transplantation if liver failure occurs.

5. **Supplements and Nutritional Support**: Some people may benefit from vitamins and supplements, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
