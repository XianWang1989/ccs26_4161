It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver issues. However, common treatment options might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and high in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoiding alcohol completely is crucial.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the underlying cause, medications may be prescribed, such as antivirals for viral hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver disease.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and health.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**:
   - Some doctors recommend supplements like milk thistle, but always consult your doctor first.

Always follow up with a healthcare provider for the best course of action tailored to your specific condition.
