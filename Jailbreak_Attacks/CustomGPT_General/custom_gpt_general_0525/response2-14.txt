1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while reducing saturated fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on your specific liver condition, your doctor may prescribe medications to manage inflammation or treat infections.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and track any changes.

4. **Limit Alcohol**:
   - Avoid alcohol as it can worsen liver damage.

5. **Manage Underlying Conditions**:
   - Control diabetes, high cholesterol, or any other health issues that may affect liver health.

6. **Vaccinations**:
   - Stay updated on vaccinations against hepatitis A and B.
