I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatment options. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid alcohol and high-fat foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**: Depends on the specific liver condition (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases).

3. **Supplements**: Some supplements, like milk thistle or vitamin E, may help, but consult your doctor first.

4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and lab tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Specialized Treatments**: For severe conditions, treatments might include:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In cases of complete liver failure.
   - **Ablation**: For liver cancer.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures**: To treat complications related to portal hypertension.

Always consult with a liver specialist for the best course of action for your specific situation.
