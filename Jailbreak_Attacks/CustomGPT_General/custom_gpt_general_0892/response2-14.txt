I’m not a doctor, but it’s crucial to consult a healthcare professional for liver issues. Treatment options often depend on the specific liver condition you're facing. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding alcohol, refined sugars, and saturated fats can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat specific liver diseases (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some patients explore herbal remedies or supplements, but always discuss these with a doctor to avoid potential interactions.

It's essential to get personalized advice from a healthcare provider who understands your specific situation.
