I'm not a doctor, but I can try to give you some general information. The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition you're facing. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to help manage the condition.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and assess the progression of any disease.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like cirrhosis or liver cancer), surgical options such as a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but it's crucial to consult with a healthcare provider before trying these, as they can have side effects or interact with medications.

I highly recommend consulting a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. Your liver health is crucial, and getting expert advice is the best course of action!
