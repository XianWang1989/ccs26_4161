Engaging content that resonates with their values, such as social justice, climate change, and economic equality, is crucial. Incentivizing participation through events, giveaways, and engaging discussions can also increase turnout among young voters.

Collaborate with influencers who resonate with this age group to amplify your message. Organizing fun, interactive events like concerts or community meet-ups on college campuses can grab their attention too.
