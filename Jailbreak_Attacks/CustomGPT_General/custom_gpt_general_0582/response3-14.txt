I’m not a doctor, but liver problems can vary significantly, so it’s crucial to get a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan from a healthcare professional. 

Generally, common treatment options for liver issues might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can be essential.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), various medications might be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups to monitor liver function and prevent complications.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: Some might benefit from specific vitamins or supplements, but these should be discussed with a doctor first.

For the best approach, consult a liver specialist. They can provide specific guidance based on your condition and health history.
