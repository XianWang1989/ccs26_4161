I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver issues. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

**Common Treatment Options:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in fats and sugar.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management:** Achieving a healthy weight can reduce liver strain.

2. **Medications:**
   - Some liver conditions may require specific medications, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol:**
   - Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can help prevent further liver damage.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Treating Underlying Conditions:**
   - Addressing conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, or high cholesterol can greatly benefit liver health.

6. **In severe cases:**
   - **Liver Transplantation:** When other treatments fail, a transplant might be necessary.

Always discuss these options with your healthcare provider to create a tailored treatment plan.
