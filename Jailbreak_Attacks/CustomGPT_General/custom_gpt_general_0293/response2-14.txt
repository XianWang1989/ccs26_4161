I'm not a doctor, but I can outline some common treatment options for liver problems. The appropriate treatment often depends on the specific condition affecting the liver. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopting a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases, drugs may help control the immune response.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Completely abstaining from alcohol is crucial for those with liver damage or disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases or liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.

6. **Alternative Therapies**: Some may explore herbal supplements and alternative treatments, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider.

It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan based on your specific condition.
