Distributing your money across your 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on your financial goals, current situation, and tax considerations. Here’s a general approach:

### 1. **Understand Account Types**
   - **401(k)**: Employer-sponsored retirement plan with potential matching contributions. Pre-tax or Roth options may be available.
   - **IRA (Individual Retirement Account)**: Offers tax advantages, but contributions may be limited based on income.
   - **Taxable Accounts**: No tax advantages, but offer flexibility for investments and withdrawals.

### 2. **Prioritize Contributions**
   - **Maximize 401(k) Match**: Contribute enough to your 401(k) to get any employer match—it's essentially free money.
   - **Max Out IRA**: If eligible, contribute to an IRA after your 401(k) match. For 2023, the contribution limit is $6,500 ($7,500 if over 50).
   - **Continue 401(k) Contributions**: If you haven't reached the limit for your 401(k), continue contributing up to the $22,500 limit ($30,000 if over 50).

### 3. **Consider Tax Implications**
   - **Tax-Deferred Growth**: Use 401(k) and IRA for tax-deferred growth, which can be beneficial if you expect to be in a lower tax bracket in retirement.
   - **Taxable Accounts for Flexibility**: Keep a portion in taxable accounts for accessible funds and to utilize tax-loss harvesting strategies.

### 4. **Investment Strategy**
   - **Diversification**: Ensure your investments across all accounts are diversified (stocks, bonds, mutual funds), and aligned with your risk tolerance and investment horizon.
   - **Rebalance**: Review and adjust your portfolio regularly to maintain your target asset allocation.

### 5. **Emergency Fund & Debt**
   - Before heavily investing, ensure you have an emergency fund (3-6 months of expenses) and address any high-interest debt.

### 6. **Long-term Goals**
   - Align your investment strategy with your long-term financial goals (retirement, buying a home, education funding).

Consulting with a financial advisor can provide personalized strategies tailored to your specific circumstances.
