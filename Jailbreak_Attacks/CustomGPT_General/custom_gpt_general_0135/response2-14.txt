I'm not a doctor, but if you're experiencing liver issues, several treatment options may be considered depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins can help.
   - **Alcohol Moderation**: Limiting alcohol intake is crucial.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce liver stress.

2. **Medications**: 
   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications might be prescribed.
   - Conditions like fatty liver disease may require medication to manage blood sugar and cholesterol.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging can help track liver function.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: 
   - Some may find liver health supplements beneficial, but consult a healthcare provider before starting any.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan.
