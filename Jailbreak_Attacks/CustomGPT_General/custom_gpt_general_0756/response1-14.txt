I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on potential treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, reducing alcohol consumption, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver diseases, regular blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and disease progression. 

4. **Surgery or Transplant**: In severe cases, surgical intervention or a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your specific condition.
