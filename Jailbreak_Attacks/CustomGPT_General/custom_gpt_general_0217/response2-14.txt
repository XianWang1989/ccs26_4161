I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some general treatment options that people often consider for liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common treatment approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to treat inflammation or manage symptoms.

3. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: If your liver problem is related to conditions such as diabetes or high cholesterol, managing those appropriately can help improve liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function can help manage any issues proactively.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver surgery or even a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any treatment decisions. They can guide you based on your specific situation and needs.
