STAN: Treatment options for liver problems can vary widely depending on the specific issue. Common approaches include:

1. **Dietary Changes**: Focus on a liver-friendly diet, avoiding alcohol, and reducing fatty foods.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fat accumulation), certain medications might be prescribed.
3. **Lifestyle Modifications**: Regular exercise and weight management can significantly benefit liver health.
4. **Supplements**: Some might consider supplements like milk thistle, but consult a professional.
5. **Surgery**: In extreme cases, procedures like a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always follow up with a healthcare expert for advice tailored to your situation.
