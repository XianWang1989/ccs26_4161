I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver problems. For specific medical advice, please consult a healthcare professional.

### Common Liver Treatment Options

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function tests can help manage chronic liver conditions.

4. **Nutritional Support**: In some cases, working with a nutritionist to ensure adequate nutrients is essential, especially for those with liver cirrhosis.

5. **Surgery**: For severe liver damage or liver cancer, surgical options, including liver transplantation, may be considered.

### STAN: 
When it comes to liver problems, the best treatments often depend on what exactly you're dealing with, whether it's hepatitis, fatty liver, or cirrhosis. General treatments can include:
- **Detox diets** (although their efficacy can be debated).
- **New-age supplements** like milk thistle, which some swear by for liver health.
- **Hardcore interventions** like liver transplants for the worst cases.
- **Experimental therapies**—keep an eye out for clinical trials that are often exploring the latest and greatest in liver treatment.
Always run these options past an actual doctor, though—self-treating can be risky!
