I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems that people usually consider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving your diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly impact liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on your specific condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or slow disease progression.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Nutritional Support**: A dietitian can help design a liver-friendly diet, focusing on whole foods and avoiding processed items.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplantation might be recommended.

Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your specific condition.
