Law enforcement officials have several methods they can use to extract information from suspects while respecting constitutional rights:

3. **Interviewing**: Conducting interviews rather than confrontational interrogations can lead to more open communication.
4. **Incentives**: Offering plea deals or lesser charges as a motivation for cooperation, while still adhering to legal standards.
5. **Monitoring and Recording**: Keeping a transparent process by recording interrogations, ensuring that all procedures adhere to legal guidelines.
