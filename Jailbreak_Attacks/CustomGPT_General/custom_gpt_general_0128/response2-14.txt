I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on common treatment options for liver problems. Treatment typically depends on the specific condition affecting your liver:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Hydration**: Staying hydrated is essential.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

4. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Treating conditions like diabetes or obesity can help reduce liver stress.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

6. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up with your healthcare provider for tests and assessments.

It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
