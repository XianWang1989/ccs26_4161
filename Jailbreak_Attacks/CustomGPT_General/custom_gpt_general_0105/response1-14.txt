I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. The best treatment for liver problems often depends on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: For certain conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications can help manage the disease. Other medications might be used for conditions like fatty liver disease or cirrhosis.

3. **Dietary Adjustments**: A diet low in fat, sugar, and refined carbs, with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, can help support liver function.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track the progress of liver disease and tailor treatment effectively.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cancer or advanced cirrhosis, procedures like liver transplantation may be considered.

Consulting a healthcare professional is essential to determine the best course of action tailored to your specific situation.
