### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet:** A balanced diet low in saturated fats, sugars, and processed foods can support liver health.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and improves liver function.

2. **Medications**
   - **Antivirals:** For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Immunosuppressants:** Used in autoimmune liver diseases to reduce immune response.

3. **Supplements**
   - **Milk Thistle:** Some studies suggest it may help protect liver cells.
   - **Vitamin E:** Can be beneficial for certain liver conditions, particularly nonalcoholic fatty liver disease.

4. **Medical Procedures**
   - **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary for liver failure.
   - **Endoscopic Procedures:** To manage complications such as varices in portal hypertension.

5. **Regular Monitoring**
   - Blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and detect any complications early.

### Conclusion
Always follow your healthcare provider's recommendations and discuss any new treatment options or changes to your current plan.
