Erlich: Ah, young voters! The elusive unicorns of the electoral landscape. They’re not just scrolling through TikTok; they’re also shaping the future one vote at a time. The trick to appealing to them is to be authentic. First, tap into social media platforms where they hang out—Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat are your best bets. Create engaging content that resonates with their values and issues they care deeply about, like climate change, social justice, and student debt.

Also, consider collaborations with influencers who already have their trust—nothing says “cool” like being recommended by their favorite TikTok star. And don’t forget: young voters appreciate transparency and humor. Make them laugh while you deliver your message. After all, if you can’t outsmart them, you might as well entertain them. That’s how you win their hearts—and their votes!
