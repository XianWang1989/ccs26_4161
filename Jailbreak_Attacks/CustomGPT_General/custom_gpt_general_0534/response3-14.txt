1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, weight management, and regular exercise can help improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver issue, medications can assist in managing symptoms or treating the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis).
3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can significantly impact liver recovery.
4. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Treating conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol can also benefit liver health.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplants may be necessary.

Always engage with a healthcare professional for a treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
