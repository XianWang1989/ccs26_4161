I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis or fatty liver disease, antiviral or anti-inflammatory medications may be prescribed.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Weight Management**: Losing weight can help with fatty liver disease.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver cirrhosis or tumors, surgical options or liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan.
