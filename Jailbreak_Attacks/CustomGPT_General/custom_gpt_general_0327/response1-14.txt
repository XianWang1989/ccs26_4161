I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems that you might discuss with your healthcare provider:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy, balanced diet, low in saturated fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis.
   - **Immunosuppressants**: For autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics**: To manage fluid retention.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to track liver function and assess damage.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Nutritional Supplements**: 
   - Some supplements may support liver health, but consult your doctor before starting any.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment options.
