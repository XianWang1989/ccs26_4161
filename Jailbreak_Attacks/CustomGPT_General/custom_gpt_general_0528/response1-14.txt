I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. Treatment options for liver problems typically depend on the specific condition you have (like fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.) and its severity. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes dietary changes, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
  
2. **Medications**: Certain liver conditions may require medications to manage symptoms or slow disease progression.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor your condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions or liver transplants may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some patients explore herbal supplements, although it's essential to consult your healthcare provider before starting any.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific liver condition.
