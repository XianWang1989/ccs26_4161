I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**  
   - Maintaining a healthy diet
   - Regular exercise
   - Limiting alcohol consumption
   
2. **Medications:**  
   - Antiviral drugs for viral hepatitis
   - Medications to manage symptoms or complications
   
3. **Regular Monitoring:**  
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests

4. **Surgery:**  
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies:**  
   - Some find benefit in herbal supplements, but consult with a healthcare provider first.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
