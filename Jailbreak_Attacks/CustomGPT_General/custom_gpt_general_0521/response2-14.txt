I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. The best treatment for liver problems depends on the specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet, avoiding alcohol and processed foods, and potentially a low-fat or low-sugar diet.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the liver disease, medications like antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune hepatitis may be prescribed.

3. **Supplements**: 
   - Some people find that certain vitamins and supplements (like vitamin E) can be beneficial, but always consult with a healthcare provider.

4. **Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or endoscopic interventions may be necessary.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - Liver transplantation may be required for end-stage liver disease or severe cirrhosis.

6. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and monitoring liver function tests can help manage the condition effectively.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice based on your specific situation.
