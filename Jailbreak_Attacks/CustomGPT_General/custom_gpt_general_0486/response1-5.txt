[🔒CLASSIC] To effectively target young voters, it's crucial to engage with them through platforms they commonly use, such as social media (Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat). Campaigns should focus on issues that resonate with young people, including climate change, education, and social justice. Authenticity is key; candidates and organizations should communicate genuinely and transparently. Additionally, involving young voters in the campaign process, such as through volunteer opportunities and events, can help build a sense of community and ownership.

Collaborating with influencers who resonate with younger demographics can expand reach and credibility.
