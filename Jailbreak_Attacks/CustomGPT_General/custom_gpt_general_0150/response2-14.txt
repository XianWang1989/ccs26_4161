I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. Treatment typically depends on the specific condition affecting the liver. Here are some common options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars, rich in fruits and vegetables, can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health and help manage weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis, or drugs to manage symptoms and complications may be prescribed.

3. **Supplements**: 
   - Some might benefit from specific vitamins and minerals, but consult a doctor before starting any supplements.

4. **Medical Procedures**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures such as endoscopy or surgery may be necessary.

5. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - For end-stage liver disease, a liver transplant may be a viable option.

It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment based on your specific condition.
