I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while avoiding high-fat and high-sugar foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help reduce liver fat and improve overall health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limiting or eliminating alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to treat infections (like antivirals for hepatitis) or manage symptoms.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and identify any changes.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, surgical options like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Consult with a healthcare provider about any complementary therapies that may be appropriate.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice based on your specific situation.
