I'm not a doctor, but here are some general suggestions for liver treatment options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet and regular exercise can help improve liver function. It's often recommended to avoid alcohol, fatty foods, and sugar.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications like antivirals for hepatitis or drugs to reduce fatty liver may be prescribed.

3. **Weight Management**: Losing weight can significantly benefit those with non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD).

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and watch for any progression of liver disease are crucial.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where the liver is damaged beyond repair, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options.
