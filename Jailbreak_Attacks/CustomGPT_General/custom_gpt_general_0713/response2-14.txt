I can't provide specific medical advice, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit saturated fats, sugars, and alcohol.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications might include antivirals for hepatitis or medications for fatty liver disease.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases (e.g., liver cirrhosis or tumors), surgical options might be necessary.

5. **Avoid Toxins**: Stay away from substances that can further damage the liver.

Consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan suited to your specific condition.
