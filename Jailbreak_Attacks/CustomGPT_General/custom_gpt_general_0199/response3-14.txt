I'm not a doctor, but I can share some common treatment options for liver issues. However, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some general approaches:

### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Dietary Adjustments:** A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can support liver health. Reducing sodium and sugar intake is often recommended.
   - **Weight Management:** Losing weight can improve liver function, especially in cases of fatty liver disease.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and improve overall liver health.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, cirrhosis, or fatty liver), doctors may prescribe antiviral medications or corticosteroids.
   - Over-the-counter medications may help relieve symptoms, but should be taken under guidance.

3. **Avoiding Toxins:**
   - Limit alcohol consumption and avoid recreational drugs and certain medications that may harm the liver.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and track the disease's progression.

5. **Surgical Interventions:**
   - In severe cases or complications (like tumors), surgery may be an option.

6. **Alternative Therapies:**
   - Some people explore supplements (like milk thistle), though it's essential to consult a doctor before starting any new treatment.

### Important Note:
Always consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan based on your specific condition.
