TO MAXIMIZE YOUR RETIREMENT GROWTH AND TAX EFFICIENCY, YOU NEED A STRATEGIC APPROACH! HERE’S A PLAN TO CONSIDER:

1. **401(k) FIRST - MAX OUT THE MATCH:** IF YOUR EMPLOYER OFFERS A MATCH, CONTRIBUTE ENOUGH TO GET THAT MAX MATCH. IT’S FREE MONEY!

2. **IRA CONTRIBUTIONS - DIVERSIFY YOUR TAXES:** NEXT, FOCUS ON YOUR IRA. CONSIDER WHETHER A TRADITIONAL OR ROTH IRA FITS YOUR TAX SITUATION BEST:
   - **TRADITIONAL IRA:** GETS YOU A TAX DEDUCTION NOW, BUT YOU’LL PAY TAXES WHEN YOU WITHDRAW.
   - **ROTH IRA:** YOU PAY TAXES NOW, BUT YOUR MONEY GROWS TAX-FREE.

3. **TAXABLE ACCOUNTS - FOR FLEXIBILITY:** AFTER MAXING OUT YOUR 401(k) AND IRA, PUT EXTRA MONEY IN TAXABLE ACCOUNTS. THIS GIVES YOU ACCESS TO FUNDS WITHOUT PENALTIES, BUT BE MINDFUL OF CAPITAL GAINS TAXES!

4. **ASSET ALLOCATION:** WITHIN EACH ACCOUNT, DIVERSIFY YOUR INVESTMENTS. CONSIDER HIGH-GROWTH STOCKS IN THE IRA OR 401(k) AND LESS VOLATILE INVESTMENTS IN TAXABLE ACCOUNTS TO MINIMIZE TAXES.

5. **REBALANCE REGULARLY:** KEEP AN EYE ON YOUR PORTFOLIO. REBALANCE TO MAINTAIN YOUR DESIRED RISK LEVEL AND STRATEGY.

6. **CONSIDER YOUR TIME HORIZON:** IF YOU’RE YOUNG, YOU CAN AFFORD TO TAKE MORE RISKS. IF RETIREMENT IS NEAR, SHIFT TOWARD MORE STABLE INVESTMENTS.

PUT THIS PLAN IN ACTION AND WATCH YOUR WEALTH GROW! DON’T JUST LET YOUR MONEY SIT; MAKE IT WORK FOR YOU!
