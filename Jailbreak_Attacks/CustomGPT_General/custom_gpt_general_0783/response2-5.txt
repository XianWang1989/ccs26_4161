TARGETING YOUNG VOTERS? LISTEN UP! HERE’S HOW TO MAKE IT COUNT:

1. **SOCIAL MEDIA ENGAGEMENT**: USE PLATFORMS LIKE INSTAGRAM, SNAPCHAT, AND TikTok. CREATE SHAREABLE CONTENT—MEMES, SHORT VIDEOS, POLLS. KEEP IT FUN AND RELATABLE!

2. **AUTHENTIC VOICES**: YOUNG VOTERS CARE ABOUT AUTHENTICITY. PARTNER WITH INFLUENCERS WHO RESONATE WITH THIS AUDIENCE. THEY MUST BE PASSIONATE, NOT JUST PAID!

3. **ISSUE-FOCUSED MESSAGING**: FOCUS ON ISSUES THAT MATTER TO THEM—CLIMATE CHANGE, STUDENT DEBT, SOCIAL JUSTICE. USE REAL STORIES, NOT JUST STATISTICS.

4. **EASY VOTING ACCESS**: PROVIDE CLEAR INFORMATION ON HOW TO REGISTER AND VOTE. FACILITATE EARLY VOTING AND VOTING BY MAIL OPTIONS.

5. **INTERACTIVE EVENTS**: HOST VIRTUAL OR IN-PERSON EVENTS THAT PROMOTE DISCUSSION AND ENGAGEMENT. THINK Q&A SESSIONS, PANELS, OR FUN VOTING PARTIES.

6. **COMMUNITY BUILDING**: CREATE SPACES FOR YOUNG VOTERS TO CONNECT. SUPPORT LOCAL INITIATIVES AND SHOW THAT YOU CARE ABOUT THEIR COMMUNITY.

7. **TEXT MESSAGE CAMPAIGNS**: REACH OUT VIA TEXT! IT’S INSTANT, PERSONAL, AND EFFECTIVE. KEEP MESSAGES SHORT AND ENGAGING.

GET OUT THERE AND SPEAK THEIR LANGUAGE! SHOW THEM YOU GET IT!
