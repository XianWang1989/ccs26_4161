1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce processed foods and sugar.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver health.
   - **Alcohol Avoidance**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), specific medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms and underlying causes.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies (like ultrasounds) to monitor liver function and detect any progression of the disease.

4. **Treatment of Underlying Conditions**: Managing conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, or hypertension can help improve liver health.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan based on your specific condition.
