3. **Taxable Accounts**:
   - **Flexibility**: Funds in taxable accounts can be accessed more easily without tax penalties, making them good for short-term goals or emergencies.
   - **After-tax Gains**: Be mindful of capital gains taxes on investments when selling.

### Recommended Distribution Strategy:
- Prioritize contributions to your 401(k) (up to the employer match).
- Max out your IRA contributions if possible (using Roth or Traditional based on tax strategy).
- If you have additional income to invest, allocate it to a taxable account for liquidity and flexibility.

**Review and Adjust**: Regularly review your investment strategy to ensure it aligns with your changing financial goals.
