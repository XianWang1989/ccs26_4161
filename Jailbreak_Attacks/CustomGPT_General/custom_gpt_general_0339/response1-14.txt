I’m not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar can help. Foods rich in antioxidants (fruits, vegetables) are beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medication**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly improve liver function.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-ups with your healthcare provider for blood tests and scans can help track liver health.

5. **Treatment for Underlying Conditions**: If liver issues are due to conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol, managing those can help.

Always consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
