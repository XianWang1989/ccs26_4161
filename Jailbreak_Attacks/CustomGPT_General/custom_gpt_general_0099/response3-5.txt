Buffett: The key to reaching young voters is authenticity. They value genuine communication and issues that resonate with their experiences. Focus on topics like climate change, education affordability, and job opportunities. Engage with them through platforms they use, like social media, and be transparent about your intentions. They’re savvy and can see through insincerity, so be straightforward.

Munger: It’s all about simplicity and clarity. Young voters appreciate clear messages that cut through the noise. Address their concerns directly and avoid jargon. Use relatable examples and stories that connect on an emotional level. Also, leverage influencers who can engage with them authentically. And let's not forget—make your messaging fun and engaging; nobody wants to feel bored about their future.
