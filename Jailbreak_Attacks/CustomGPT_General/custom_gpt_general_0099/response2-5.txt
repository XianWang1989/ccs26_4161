Targeting young voters effectively requires a blend of authenticity, engagement, and alignment with their values.

**Buffett:** Young voters are often motivated by issues they care about deeply, such as climate change, education reform, and social justice. The key is to communicate honestly and clearly about these issues. Craft a narrative that demonstrates not just promises, but also a track record of integrity and results. It's all about building trust. If they see candidates as genuine and relatable, they’ll be more inclined to participate.

**Munger:** You have to meet them where they are. Use social media platforms that resonate with this demographic, like Instagram or TikTok, because that's where their attention is. Create content that’s not just informative but entertaining and engaging. Also, involve them in the conversation—ask for their input and ideas. Young people respond well when they feel they have a voice and ownership in the process.
