I'm not a doctor, but there are several common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition (e.g., fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis). Here are some general options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Dietary Modifications**: A healthy, balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reducing sugar, salt, and saturated fats can help.
   - **Weight Management**: For overweight individuals, gradual weight loss can reduce fat in the liver.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause (e.g., antiviral medications for hepatitis).

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for many liver conditions.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular checkups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and assess the progression of any liver disease.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as cirrhosis or cancer, procedures like liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options based on your specific situation.
