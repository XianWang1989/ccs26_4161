It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice regarding liver issues. However, some common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), doctors might prescribe antiviral medications or specific treatments to manage symptoms.
3. **Nutritional Support**: Dietitian-recommended meal plans can support liver function and overall health.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests are crucial for tracking liver health and adjusting treatments as needed.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases or conditions like liver cancer, surgical options, including liver transplantation, may be considered.

Always discuss with a healthcare provider to determine the most appropriate course of action for your situation.
