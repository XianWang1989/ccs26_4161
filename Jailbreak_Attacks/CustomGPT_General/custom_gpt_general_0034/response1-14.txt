I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver health. The best treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition you're dealing with (such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, etc.). Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and maintaining a healthy weight to improve liver function.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed. For example, antiviral medications for viral hepatitis or immunosuppressants for autoimmune liver disease.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

4. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Treating conditions like diabetes, high cholesterol, or obesity can also help improve liver health.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function may be necessary.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplants may be considered.

It’s crucial to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide a diagnosis and tailor a treatment plan specific to your needs.
