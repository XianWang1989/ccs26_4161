### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid high-fat, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water to help the liver function properly.
   - **Alcohol:** Avoid alcohol, as it can worsen liver problems.

2. **Medications:**
   - **Antiviral Drugs:** For viral hepatitis, medications can help manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids:** Sometimes used for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements:** 
   - Certain vitamins, like vitamin E, have been studied for liver health, but always consult your doctor before starting any supplements.

4. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and assess damage.

5. **Procedures:**
   - In cases of significant liver damage or cirrhosis, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

6. **Management of Complications:**
   - Treating related conditions like diabetes, obesity, or high cholesterol that can impact liver health.
