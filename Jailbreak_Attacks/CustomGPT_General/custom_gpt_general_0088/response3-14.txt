**Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Low-fat, high-fiber diet; avoiding alcohol.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the specific liver issue, medications might be prescribed to manage conditions like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis.

3. **Monitoring:**
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Interventional Procedures:**
   - In severe cases, procedures such as liver biopsy or endoscopy may be needed.

5. **Transplant:**
   - For end-stage liver disease, a liver transplant might be considered.

**Alternative Therapies:**
- Some explore herbal supplements, but these should only be used under medical advice, as some can be harmful.

Again, it’s essential to discuss your symptoms and medical history with a healthcare professional for the best course of action.
