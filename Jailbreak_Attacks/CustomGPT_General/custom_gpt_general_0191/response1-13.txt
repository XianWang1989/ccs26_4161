[🔒CLASSIC] The best way to distribute your money among your 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on several factors, including your financial goals, tax situation, and investment timeline. Generally, it’s advisable to maximize contributions to your 401(k) especially if your employer offers a matching contribution, as this is effectively "free money."
