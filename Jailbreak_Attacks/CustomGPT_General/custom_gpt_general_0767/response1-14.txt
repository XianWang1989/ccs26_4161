I'm not a doctor, but if you're experiencing liver issues, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Common treatment options for liver problems might include:

1. **Medications**: Specific medications depending on the liver condition (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
2. **Lifestyle changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can support liver health.
3. **Weight management**: For fatty liver disease, losing weight can be beneficial.
4. **Regular monitoring**: Routine check-ups to track liver function and disease progression.

Always seek professional medical guidance for your specific situation.
