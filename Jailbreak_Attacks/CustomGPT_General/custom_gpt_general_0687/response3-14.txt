I'm not a doctor, but I can provide general information. For liver issues, treatment options vary based on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Common approaches include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications (low fat, high fiber), regular exercise, and weight management.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications to manage symptoms or slow disease progression.
3. **Limit Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can significantly help liver health.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan.

STAN: If you have liver problems, consider trying a strict detox diet, like intermittent fasting or juice cleanses—though science is mixed on those. Some swear by herbal supplements, like milk thistle, but check with a doc first. If things get serious, a liver specialist (hepatologist) is the way to go. They might suggest experimental treatments or even a liver transplant if needed. Always push for the latest options!
