I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition. Here they are:

- **Lifestyle Modifications**: 
  - Adopt a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  - Engage in regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

- **Medication**: 
  - Depending on the liver condition (such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or halt disease progression.
  
- **Avoid Alcohol and Toxins**: 
  - Completely abstain from alcohol and minimize exposure to environmental toxins.

- **Regular Monitoring**: 
  - Schedule regular check-ups with a healthcare provider for ongoing assessment and management of liver health.

- **Potential Surgical Options**: 
  - In severe cases, such as liver cirrhosis or tumors, surgical intervention or liver transplantation may be necessary.

- **Supplements and Herbal Remedies**: 
  - Some may benefit from liver-supportive supplements, but consult with a healthcare professional before starting anything.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific liver issue.
