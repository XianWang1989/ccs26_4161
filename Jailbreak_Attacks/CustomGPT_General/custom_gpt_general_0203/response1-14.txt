I'm not a healthcare professional, but if you're experiencing liver problems, it’s crucial to consult with a doctor for accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment. Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Adopting a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - Avoiding alcohol and reducing saturated fats.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors might prescribe medications to manage symptoms or reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Regular Check-ups**: Monitoring liver function through blood tests can help track the progress of treatment.

4. **Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or surgery may be necessary.

5. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: If the liver issue is related to conditions like hepatitis, diabetes, or fatty liver disease, focusing on managing those conditions is crucial.

Always seek professional medical advice for the best course of action regarding your liver health.
