I'm not a doctor, but I can share some general information about liver treatment options. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, and reducing alcohol intake can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), doctors may prescribe antivirals or other medications to treat underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests help track liver function and are crucial for managing liver disease.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as a liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: Some people find herbal supplements help, but these should be used cautiously and discussed with a doctor.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
