I'm not a doctor, but treatment options for liver problems can vary depending on the specific condition. Generally, they may include:

1. **Medications**: To manage symptoms or underlying causes, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
2. **Lifestyle changes**: Including a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol.
3. **Nutritional support**: Sometimes, dietary supplements may be recommended.
4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and imaging tests to assess liver function.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan. 

If you have specific symptoms or a diagnosis, please share that for more focused information.
