1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars, and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoiding alcohol can prevent further damage.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, various medications might be prescribed to manage symptoms or underlying causes (e.g., antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups with liver function tests to monitor the disease's progression.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, surgery or procedures like liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Supplements**: 
   - Some people may use supplements like milk thistle, although the evidence of their effectiveness can vary.

Always prioritize a discussion with a healthcare professional to determine what’s best for your specific condition.
