I'm not a doctor, but common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce symptoms or treat underlying issues (e.g., antivirals for viral hepatitis).
3. **Therapies**: In cases like fatty liver disease, specific therapies aimed at improving liver function may be recommended.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

Consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action tailored to your specific situation.
