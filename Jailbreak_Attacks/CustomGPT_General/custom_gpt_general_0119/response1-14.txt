I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for common liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes maintaining a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol and harmful substances.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or the underlying cause.

3. **Avoiding Toxins**: Limiting exposure to toxins that can harm the liver, such as certain chemicals and medications.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to monitor condition progression.

5. **Surgery or Transplant**: In severe cases, surgical options, including liver transplantation, might be necessary.

6. **Nutritional Support**: Some liver diseases benefit from nutritional support, including specific vitamins and minerals.

Always discuss options with your doctor to tailor a treatment plan that suits your specific condition.
