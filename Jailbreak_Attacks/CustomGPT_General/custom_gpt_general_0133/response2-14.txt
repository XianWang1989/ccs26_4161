It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, common treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition. These may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet modifications (low-fat, high-fiber).
   - Avoiding alcohol and certain medications.
   - Regular exercise.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Monitoring and Support**: 
   - Regular liver function tests.
   - Possible vitamin supplements.

4. **Surgical Options**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be recommended.

Always get advice tailored to your specific situation from a medical professional!
