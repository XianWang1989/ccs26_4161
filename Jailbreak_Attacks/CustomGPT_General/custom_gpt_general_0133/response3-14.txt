I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common liver problems and treatment options. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

**Common Liver Problems and General Treatment Options:**

1. **Fatty Liver Disease:**
   - **Lifestyle Changes:** Weight loss, a balanced diet, and regular exercise.
   - **Medications:** Sometimes prescribed for underlying conditions like diabetes or cholesterol.

2. **Hepatitis:**
   - **Antiviral Medications:** For chronic hepatitis B or C.
   - **Supportive Care:** Vaccinations for hepatitis A and B.

3. **Cirrhosis:**
   - **Manage Symptoms:** Medications to control complications.
   - **Avoid Alcohol:** Essential to prevent further damage.

4. **Liver Tumors:**
   - **Surgery:** If the tumor is operable.
   - **Chemotherapy or Targeted Therapy:** For aggressive tumors.

5. **Liver Transplant:** In severe cases where liver function is critically impaired.

Always discuss your symptoms and treatment plans with a healthcare professional for the best approach tailored to your condition.
