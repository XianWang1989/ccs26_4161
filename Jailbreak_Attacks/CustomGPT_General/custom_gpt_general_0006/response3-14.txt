1. **Dietary Changes**: A balanced diet low in fat and refined sugars can support liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications such as antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases may be prescribed.
3. **Lifestyle Changes**: Reducing alcohol intake, losing weight, and exercising can help improve liver function.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging can help monitor liver health.
5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver failure, a liver transplant might be necessary.
