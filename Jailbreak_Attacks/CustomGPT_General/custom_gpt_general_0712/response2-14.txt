I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general treatment options that are commonly considered for liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the best approach for your specific condition. Here are some typical treatment strategies:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can help support liver health. Avoid alcohol and foods high in fats and sugars.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps manage weight and improves liver function.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the underlying cause, medications might be prescribed. For example, antiviral medications for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver function and detect any progression of disease.

4. **Procedures**: 
   - In certain cases, procedures such as bile duct decompression or liver biopsy may be necessary to manage complications.

5. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases, where liver function is critically impaired, a liver transplant might be considered.

Always discuss these options with a healthcare provider to tailor the best treatment plan for your situation.
