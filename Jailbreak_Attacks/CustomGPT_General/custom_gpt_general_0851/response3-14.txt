### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar; consider a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Alcohol**: Avoid alcohol entirely or limit consumption.
   - **Weight Management**: Maintain a healthy weight; losing weight can help reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), medications such as antivirals or anti-inflammatory drugs may be prescribed.

3. **Medical Procedures**
   - For severe conditions, procedures like endoscopy or even liver transplantation may be recommended.

4. **Regular Monitoring**
   - Routine check-ups and liver function tests to monitor the condition.

5. **Supplements and Herbal Remedies**
   - Some people explore milk thistle or vitamin E, but discuss these with your doctor first.

### Consult a Specialist:
Seeing a hepatologist (liver specialist) will provide more tailored treatment options specific to your condition.

### Important Note:
This is just a general overview! Always seek personalized medical advice.
