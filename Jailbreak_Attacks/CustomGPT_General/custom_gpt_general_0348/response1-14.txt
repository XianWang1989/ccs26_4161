I’m not a doctor, but I can suggest some general treatment options that are commonly considered for liver problems. However, it is crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored advice based on your specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the diagnosis, medications may include antiviral drugs for hepatitis, corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases, or medications for specific conditions affecting the liver.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Frequent monitoring by a healthcare provider can help in tracking the progression of liver disease and adjusting treatment as necessary.

4. **Avoiding Toxins**: Steering clear of substances that can harm the liver, such as certain medications or industrial chemicals.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, if the liver is significantly damaged, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for advice tailored to your conditions.
