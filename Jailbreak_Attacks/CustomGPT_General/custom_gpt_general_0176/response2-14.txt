I'm really sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for tailored advice, as they can provide a proper diagnosis and treatment plan based on your specific condition. Common treatment options for liver issues can include lifestyle changes, medications, and sometimes procedures or surgery, depending on the severity and type of liver problem. 

Some general recommendations might include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet low in saturated fats and sugars, along with regular exercise.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.
3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption can significantly improve liver health.
4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups to monitor liver function.
5. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation might be considered.

Always consult your doctor or a liver specialist for the best approach for your situation. Take care!
