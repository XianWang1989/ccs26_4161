I'm not a doctor, but there are several common treatment approaches for liver problems that you might consider discussing with a healthcare professional:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause of the liver problem, medications may be prescribed. This can include antiviral drugs for hepatitis or drugs to reduce liver inflammation.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function are crucial, especially if you have chronic liver disease.

4. **Supplements**: Some people find liver-supportive supplements (like milk thistle) beneficial, but it's essential to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplements.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions like a liver transplant may be necessary if the liver is severely damaged.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
