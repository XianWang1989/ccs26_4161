I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some general treatment options for liver problems. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized advice. Common treatment options include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - Adopting a healthy diet, low in fats and sugars.
   - Maintaining a healthy weight.
   - Avoiding alcohol and recreational drugs.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Routine blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Supplements**:
   - Some people may benefit from supplements, but it’s crucial to consult a healthcare provider before starting any.

5. **Liver Transplant**:
   - In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare provider for advice tailored to your specific situation.
