It's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, but generally, treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Maintaining a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Avoiding alcohol and minimizing consumption of processed foods.
   - Regular exercise.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may include antivirals for hepatitis, steroids for autoimmune liver disease, or drugs to manage symptoms and complications.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver function and health.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver biopsy or even liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some individuals explore herbal supplements, but these should only be taken under medical supervision.

Always consult with your healthcare provider to determine the best course of action tailored to your specific situation.
