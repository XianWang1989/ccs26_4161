I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice regarding liver problems. However, common treatment options for liver issues may include:

1. **Medications**: Depending on the specific condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver), antiviral or corticosteroid medications might be prescribed.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver recovery.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests can help monitor liver function and guide treatment.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but these should only be taken after discussing with a healthcare provider.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplantation may be necessary.

Always seek advice from a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and appropriate treatment options specific to your situation.
