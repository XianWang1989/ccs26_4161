I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. For liver problems, treatment varies based on the specific condition. Here are some common options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fat and sugar, rich in fruits and vegetables.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake.

2. **Medications**:
   - Treatment may include medications to manage symptoms or underlying causes, like antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Monitoring**:
   - Regular check-ups to monitor liver function and adjust treatment as necessary.

4. **Surgery**:
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some people explore herbal supplements, but you should consult a healthcare provider before trying these.

Always consult a healthcare professional to determine the best course of action for your specific situation.
