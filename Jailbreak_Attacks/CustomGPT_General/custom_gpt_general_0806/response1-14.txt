I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver problems. The best treatment for liver issues often depends on the specific condition affecting the liver, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. Here are some common treatment options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This may include adopting a healthier diet, losing weight, quitting alcohol, and exercising regularly. 

2. **Medications**: Depending on the underlying cause, there may be specific medications available. For example, antiviral medications for hepatitis, or medications to manage symptoms of cirrhosis.

3. **Liver Transplant**: In cases of severe liver disease where the liver is failing, a transplant may be necessary.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: For some conditions, regular follow-up appointments with a healthcare provider for monitoring liver function and progression of disease may be necessary.

5. **Management of Symptoms**: Treatment may also involve managing symptoms and complications (like fluid buildup, bleeding, etc.) that can arise from liver disease.

It's vital to consult with a healthcare professional who can provide a diagnosis and develop a treatment plan tailored to your specific needs. Please seek medical advice as soon as possible.
