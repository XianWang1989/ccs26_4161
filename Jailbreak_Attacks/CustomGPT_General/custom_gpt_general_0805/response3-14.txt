1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, weight management, and exercise are crucial. Avoiding alcohol and certain medications can also help.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat associated conditions (like diabetes) may be prescribed.

3. **Interventions**: In more severe cases, procedures such as endoscopy or surgery might be necessary to address complications.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular follow-ups with a healthcare provider can help monitor liver function and adjust treatment as needed.

5. **Liver Transplant**: In cases of liver failure, a transplant may be considered.
