I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help improve liver function.
   - **Limit Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can significantly aid liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, medications like interferon or direct-acting antivirals may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some may benefit from herbal supplements, but always consult your doctor first.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional to determine the best treatment tailored to your specific condition.
