### Common Liver Treatment Options

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid saturated fats, sugar, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: For viral hepatitis, such as hepatitis B or C.
   - **Anti-inflammatory Drugs**: Corticosteroids might be prescribed for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Supplements**:
   - **Milk Thistle**: Some studies suggest it may support liver health.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver disease, a transplant may be necessary.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Continuous check-ups and blood tests are vital to assess liver function and monitor disease progression.

### Important Notes
- Avoid alcohol and limit exposure to toxins.
- Stay hydrated and manage chronic conditions like diabetes.

Ultimately, treatment options depend on the specific liver condition, so speak with a healthcare provider for tailored recommendations.
