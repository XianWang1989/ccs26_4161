I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for a personalized approach. Here are some common treatment options depending on the specific liver issue:

1. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications can be effective. Other liver diseases might require different medications, such as corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

3. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: Treating related issues like diabetes or obesity can help in managing liver health.

4. **Procedures**: In severe cases, treatments may include procedures such as liver dialysis or, in extreme instances, liver transplantation.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver function and detect any complications early.

Always seek guidance from a healthcare provider for tailored advice and treatment options.
