I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information regarding liver health. It's important to consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan. 

Treatment options for liver problems often depend on the underlying condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet low in fat and sugar, maintaining a healthy weight, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or cure the disease.

3. **Minimally Invasive Procedures**: In some cases, procedures such as endoscopy may be used to address complications like varices, which can arise from liver disease.

4. **Surgery**: For serious conditions, such as liver tumors or significant damage, surgery may be necessary, including liver transplant in severe cases.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood work to monitor liver function and condition progression are crucial.

Again, it's essential to discuss these options with your healthcare provider, who can tailor advice based on your specific health needs. Feel free to ask more questions if you need further information!
