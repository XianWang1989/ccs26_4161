**Common Treatment Options for Liver Issues:**

1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - Diet: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Alcohol: Avoid alcohol to reduce liver strain.
   - Weight Management: Achieving a healthy weight can improve liver function.

2. **Medications:**
   - Antivirals: For viral hepatitis.
   - Corticosteroids: To reduce inflammation.
   - Diuretics: For fluid retention in liver disease.

3. **Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups with liver function tests and imaging.

4. **Surgery:**
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies:**
   - Supplements like milk thistle may provide some support, but consult your doctor before use.

Always seek professional guidance for a treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
