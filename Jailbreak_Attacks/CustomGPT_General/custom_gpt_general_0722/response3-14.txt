### Treatment Options for Liver Problems

#### 1. **Medication**
   - **Antiviral drugs** for hepatitis
   - **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases

#### 2. **Lifestyle Changes**
   - **Diet**: Focus on a balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and salt.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

#### 3. **Avoiding Alcohol**
   - Completely abstaining from alcohol; it can worsen liver damage.

#### 4. **Weight Management**
   - Losing weight if overweight can reduce fatty liver issues.

#### 5. **Regular Monitoring**
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

#### 6. **Surgery**
   - In severe cases, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
