It's important to consult a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plan for liver problems. Treatment options often depend on the specific condition but may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: A healthy diet, regular exercise, and weight management can support liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications can help manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.
3. **Avoidance of Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can significantly improve liver function.
4. **Vaccinations**: Getting vaccinated against hepatitis A and B may be recommended.
5. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function.

Please consult a doctor for a personalized approach.
