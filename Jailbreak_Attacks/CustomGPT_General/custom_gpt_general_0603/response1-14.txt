I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest that if you're experiencing liver problems, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment options. Treatment for liver conditions can vary widely depending on the specific issue, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver cancer. 

Generally, treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Certain medications may be prescribed to treat underlying causes of liver issues, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: For chronic liver diseases, regular check-ups and monitoring of liver function can help manage the condition.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options may be necessary, like liver transplant for end-stage liver disease.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some individuals explore supplements or alternative therapies, but these should be discussed with a healthcare provider to avoid potential harm.

Always seek professional medical advice for personalized recommendations.
