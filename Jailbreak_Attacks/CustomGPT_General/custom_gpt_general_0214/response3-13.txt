To optimize your investment distribution among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts, consider these strategies:

### GPT:
1. **Maximize Tax-Advantaged Accounts:**

   - **IRA:** Fund a traditional or Roth IRA next, depending on your tax situation and whether you prefer tax-deferred growth or tax-free withdrawals.

2. **Asset Allocation:**
   - Place more tax-efficient investments (like stocks or index funds) in taxable accounts.
   - Consider holding higher taxed investments (like bonds) in tax-advantaged accounts.

3. **Rebalancing:**
   - Regularly review and rebalance your portfolio to maintain your desired asset allocation across all accounts.

4. **Withdrawal Strategy:**
   - Plan your withdrawals strategically in retirement to minimize taxes, considering which account(s) to withdraw from first based on your tax bracket.
