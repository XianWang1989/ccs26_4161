I'm not a doctor, but I can provide general information about common liver problems and treatment options. Treatment often depends on the specific condition affecting the liver. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars can help. Foods rich in antioxidants may support liver health.
   - Exercise: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed.
   - Supplements for liver support, such as milk thistle, may also be suggested but discuss them with your doctor first.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Completely abstaining from alcohol can be crucial for liver health, especially in cases of alcoholic liver disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies may be necessary to monitor liver function and progression of disease.

5. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, liver transplant may be considered for end-stage liver disease.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
