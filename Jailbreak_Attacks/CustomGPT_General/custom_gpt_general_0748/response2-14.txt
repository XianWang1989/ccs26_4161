I'm not a doctor, but I can offer general information about liver treatments. Options typically depend on the specific liver condition. Here are some common treatments:

1. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications can help. Other liver diseases may require steroids or immunosuppressants.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a balanced diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can improve liver health.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, liver transplantation may be an option.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Supplements like milk thistle are sometimes considered, but consult a doctor before trying any alternative treatments.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a personalized approach based on your specific condition.
