### GPT:
1. **Prioritize Contributions**:

   - **IRA**: Maximize contributions to IRA accounts (Traditional or Roth) based on your tax situation and eligibility.
   - **Taxable Accounts**: Use these for additional savings after maximizing tax-advantaged accounts.

2. **Consider Tax Implications**:
   - **Tax-Deferred Accounts**: Like 401(k)s and Traditional IRAs, grow tax-deferred until withdrawal.
   - **Roth Accounts**: Contributions are made after-tax, providing tax-free growth and withdrawals in retirement if rules are followed.
   - **Taxable Accounts**: Investments in taxed only when gains are realized; suitable for shorter-term goals.

3. **Liquid Needs**: Ensure you have enough in taxable accounts for short-term expenses without penalties.

4. **Investment Allocation**: Diversify investments based on risk tolerance and time horizon within each account.

5. **Review and Adjust**: Regularly review your allocations and adjust based on performance, tax laws, and life changes.
