I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver health. Treatment options for liver problems can vary based on the specific condition, such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, or cirrhosis. Common approaches may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Losing weight if overweight.
   - Maintaining a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - Reducing alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral drugs for hepatitis.
   - Medications to manage blood pressure or cholesterol levels.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function.

4. **Exercise**: 
   - Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.

5. **Avoiding Toxins**: 
   - Limiting exposure to environmental toxins and harmful substances.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment.
