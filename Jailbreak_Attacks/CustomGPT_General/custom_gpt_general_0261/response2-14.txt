I’m not a doctor, but I can give you some general information about common liver treatment options. The best approach depends on the specific liver condition you have. Here are some common treatments:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet low in fat and sugar can help. Foods rich in antioxidants (like fruits and vegetables) are beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight and liver function.
   - **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals**: If you have viral hepatitis, antiviral medications can manage the infection.
   - **Corticosteroids**: Used for autoimmune liver diseases to reduce inflammation.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine blood tests and imaging studies can track liver health and function.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases (like liver cancer or cirrhosis), surgical options may include liver transplantation.

5. **Supportive Care**: 
   - Management of symptoms like jaundice or ascites can improve quality of life.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific situation.
