I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about common liver problems and treatment options. Treatment depends on the specific condition affecting your liver, such as:

1. **Fatty Liver Disease**: Lifestyle changes like weight loss, a healthy diet, regular exercise, and controlling blood sugar levels may be recommended.

2. **Hepatitis**: Antiviral medications can help treat viral hepatitis (A, B, or C). For autoimmune hepatitis, corticosteroids or immunosuppressants may be prescribed.

3. **Cirrhosis**: Managing the underlying cause (like alcohol consumption or hepatitis) is crucial, and in severe cases, a liver transplant might be necessary.

4. **Liver Cancer**: Treatments can include surgery, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, or chemotherapy.

5. **Liver Enzyme Elevation**: Treating the cause of the elevated enzymes, such as stopping medications that are harmful to the liver, is essential.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition.
