I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding alcohol can significantly help liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, or cirrhosis), doctors may prescribe antiviral medications, corticosteroids, or other drugs.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function and performance through blood tests and imaging as necessary.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical options such as liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: Treating diabetes, high cholesterol, or other related health issues can improve liver health.

6. **Supplements and Herbal Remedies**: Some people look into milk thistle and other supplements, but it’s crucial to discuss these with a healthcare provider.

It’s essential to consult with a medical professional to determine the best treatment based on your specific diagnosis and health status.
