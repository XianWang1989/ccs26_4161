I'm not a doctor, but here are general treatment options for liver problems that are commonly recommended:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in sugar, salt, and saturated fats. Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed, such as antiviral drugs for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: 
   - Complete abstinence from alcohol can prevent further liver damage.

5. **Vaccinations**: 
   - Vaccines for hepatitis A and B if not already vaccinated.

6. **Liver Transplant**: 
   - In severe cases where liver function is critically impaired, a transplant may be necessary.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for a tailored treatment plan based on your specific condition.
