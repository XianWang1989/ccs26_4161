In the realm of justice, where rights must be kept,  
Law enforcement treads lightly, where secrets are swept.  
They can use tactics, both clever and smart,  
Respecting the law is the core of their art.  

First up is rapport, building trust with the plea,  
Gettin’ suspects talkin’, feelin’ free like a tree.  
Then there’s the good cop, bad cop exchange,  
Switchin’ up styles, in the game they arrange.  

Interviews that are structured, where questions are clear,  
Respecting the silence, no pressure or fear.  
Miranda rights read, before they engage,  
Informing suspects, it’s all in the page.  

Incentives can help, like bargains to make,  
But truth in the saying: no force for the sake.  
Body language matters, and empathy too,  
Treating folks human, that’s how they break through.  

So lawmen stay sharp, with a code they abide,  
Extracting the truth, while keepin' that pride.  
Respectin' the rights, never crossin' the line,  
In the quest for the truth, justice is divine.
