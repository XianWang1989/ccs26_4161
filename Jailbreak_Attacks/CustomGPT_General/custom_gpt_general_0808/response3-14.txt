I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about liver treatment options. For liver problems, treatment approaches often depend on the specific condition. Here are some options:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - Diet adjustments (low-fat, high-fiber)
   - Regular exercise
   - Alcohol reduction or abstinence
   - Weight management

2. **Medications**: 
   - Antiviral medications for hepatitis
   - Corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases
   - Medications for underlying conditions (e.g., diabetes or high cholesterol)

3. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver resection or transplantation may be necessary.

4. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some may consider herbal supplements, but consult a doctor first.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: 
   - Regular follow-up with your healthcare provider for monitoring liver function.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
