### Common Treatment Options for Liver Problems

1. **Medications**:
   - **Antivirals** for hepatitis.
   - **Corticosteroids** for autoimmune liver diseases.
   - **Diuretics** to manage fluid retention.

2. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Dietary Modifications**: Adopt a liver-friendly diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit alcohol, sugar, and saturated fats.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management**: Losing weight can help reduce liver fat and improve function.

3. **Surgery**:
   - **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases of liver failure, a transplant may be necessary.
   - **Surgical Procedures**: To remove tumors or bile duct obstructions.

4. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - **Herbal Supplements**: Some people use milk thistle or turmeric, but always consult with a doctor first.

5. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Keeping track of liver function through blood tests and imaging studies.

### Important Notes
- **Diagnosis**: Accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment. 
- **Consultation**: Always consult a healthcare provider for tailored treatment options specific to your condition.
