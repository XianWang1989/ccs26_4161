I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information that might be helpful. The best treatment options for liver problems vary widely based on the specific condition and its severity. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Modifying your diet to include more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while reducing sugar, fats, and alcohol consumption can be beneficial. Regular exercise and weight management are also important.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the nature of the liver issue, medications may be prescribed. For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be necessary.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: If alcohol is a factor in your liver problems, abstaining from alcohol is crucial. 

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Keeping regular appointments with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function is essential for many liver conditions.

5. **Surgery or Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, especially with end-stage liver disease or liver failure, surgical options like a liver transplant might be necessary.

It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan. They can provide the most accurate information based on your specific situation.
