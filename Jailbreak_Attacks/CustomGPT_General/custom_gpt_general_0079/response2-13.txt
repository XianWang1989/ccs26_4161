Distributing your money among a 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts depends on several factors, including your financial goals, tax situation, and retirement timeline. Here’s a general approach to consider:

### 1. **Maximize Employer Match in 401(k)**
   - **Prioritize contributions** to your 401(k) up to the employer match, as this is essentially “free money.”

### 2. **Contribute to an IRA**
   - If you’re eligible, consider contributing to a Roth IRA for tax-free growth and withdrawals in retirement or a Traditional IRA for tax-deferred growth.
   - Aim to maximize your IRA contributions if you have additional funds after your 401(k).

### 3. **Pay Off High-Interest Debt**
   - Before focusing heavily on investing, consider paying off high-interest debt which can provide a guaranteed return.

### 4. **Continue Funding Your 401(k)**
   - After maximizing contributions to your IRA, direct any additional funds into your 401(k) until you reach the annual contribution limit.

### 5. **Taxable Accounts for Flexibility**
   - Any remaining funds can be directed to a taxable brokerage account. This allows for flexibility and access to funds if needed before retirement.

### 6. **Consider Your Tax Bracket**
   - Evaluate your current and expected future tax rates. If you anticipate being in a lower tax bracket in retirement, a Traditional IRA might be beneficial. Conversely, if you expect a higher rate, a Roth IRA could be more advantageous.

### 7. **Diversify Investments**
   - Regardless of account type, ensure you have a diversified portfolio across equities, bonds, and other asset classes based on your risk tolerance.

### 8. **Review and Adjust Regularly**
   - Regularly review your asset allocation and contributions in response to changes in financial goals, market conditions, and life events.

### Conclusion
The best distribution strategy combines maximizing employer benefits, leveraging tax advantages, and maintaining flexibility for liquidity. Consider consulting with a financial advisor for personalized advice tailored to your specific situation.
