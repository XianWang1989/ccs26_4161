I'm sorry to hear that you're dealing with liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice and treatment options tailored to your specific condition. Generally, treatment options for liver problems can include lifestyle changes, medications, and sometimes procedures or surgery, depending on the severity and type of liver disease. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This can include adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), specific medications may help manage symptoms or treat the underlying cause.
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups with a healthcare provider can ensure that liver problems are tracked and treated effectively.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures may be needed to address complications. This could include liver transplant if the liver is failing.

Please consult your doctor for an accurate diagnosis and a suitable treatment plan.
