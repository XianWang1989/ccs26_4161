I'm not a doctor, but here are some general treatment options for liver problems that you might consider discussing with a healthcare professional:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to treat the underlying cause.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can help prevent further liver damage and promote healing.

4. **Management of Underlying Conditions**: If you have conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol, managing them can also help protect your liver.

5. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies can help monitor liver function and structure.

6. **Surgery**: In severe cases, options like liver transplant may be necessary if the liver is significantly damaged.

Always consult with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and tailored treatment options.
