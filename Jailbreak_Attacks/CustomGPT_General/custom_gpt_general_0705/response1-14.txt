I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems, which can depend on the specific condition:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding alcohol can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, medications such as antivirals or immunosuppressants may be needed.

3. **Weight Management**: If applicable, losing weight can help with fatty liver disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Supplementation**: Certain vitamins and supplements may support liver health, but consult a healthcare provider first.

6. **Liver Transplant**: For severe liver damage or disease, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a tailored plan based on your specific condition.
