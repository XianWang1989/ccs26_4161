I'm not a doctor, but I can share some common treatment options for liver problems, which usually depend on the specific condition. Here are a few:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Diet modifications, exercise, and weight management can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: For conditions like hepatitis, antiviral medications may be prescribed. Other liver diseases might require different medications to manage symptoms or improve liver function.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function may be necessary.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption is crucial for liver health.

5. **Vaccinations**: Vaccines for hepatitis A and B can help prevent these infections, especially if you have liver disease.

6. **Surgery or Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your specific condition.
