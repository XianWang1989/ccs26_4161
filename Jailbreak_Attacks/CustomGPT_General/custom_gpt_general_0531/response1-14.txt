I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on liver treatment options. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some common treatment options for various liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A healthy diet low in alcohol, saturated fats, and sugars can help.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve overall liver function.
   - **Weight Management**: Achieving a healthy weight is crucial, especially for fatty liver disease.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver condition, medicines may target inflammation, viral infections (like antiviral drugs for hepatitis), or jaundice.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**:
   - Regular blood tests to monitor liver enzymes and function can help track the progression of liver disease.

4. **Surgery or Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, treatments like liver transplant may be necessary.
   - Non-invasive procedures can be used to remove blockages or tumors.

5. **Alternative Therapies**:
   - Some patients explore herbal remedies and supplements, but these should only be used under medical guidance, as they can interact with conventional treatments.

Make sure to discuss your symptoms and medical history with a healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment plan.
