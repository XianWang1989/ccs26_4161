I'm not a doctor, but here are some commonly recommended treatment options for liver problems, depending on the specific issue:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid high-fat, high-sugar, and processed foods.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.
   - **Alcohol**: Limit or eliminate alcohol consumption, as it can worsen liver problems.

2. **Medications**:
   - **Antiviral Drugs**: For viral hepatitis, medications like tenofovir or entecavir may be prescribed.
   - **Corticosteroids**: For autoimmune liver diseases, steroids may help reduce inflammation.
   - **Liver Supplements**: Some patients benefit from supplements like milk thistle, but consult your doctor first.

3. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Schedule regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function tests and manage your condition.

4. **Surgical Options**:
   - In severe cases of liver damage (like cirrhosis), a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Management of Underlying Conditions**:
   - Control conditions that can affect liver health, such as diabetes, high cholesterol, or obesity.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your specific condition.
