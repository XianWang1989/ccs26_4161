I'm not a doctor, but if you're dealing with liver issues, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. However, here are some common treatment options that healthcare providers might consider for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving diet by reducing alcohol intake, eating a balanced diet, and maintaining a healthy weight can significantly aid liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (like antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging may be required to monitor liver function and progression of the disease.

4. **Supplemental Therapies**: Certain supplements like milk thistle have been explored, but you should discuss these with your doctor.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, such as liver failure or tumors, surgical options may be necessary, including a liver transplant.

Always follow up with a healthcare provider for a diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
