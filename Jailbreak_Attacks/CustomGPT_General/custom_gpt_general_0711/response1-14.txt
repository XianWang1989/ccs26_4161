I'm not a doctor, but there are several treatment options available for liver problems depending on the specific condition you have. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Improving diet and exercise can help manage liver health. Reducing alcohol intake and avoiding hepatotoxic substances are also critical.

2. **Medications**: Certain liver diseases may be treated with medications. For instance, antiviral drugs are used for viral hepatitis, while steroids can help in autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Keeping regular follow-up appointments with a healthcare provider can help track the progression of liver disease and the effectiveness of treatments.

4. **Dietary Supplements**: Some people consider supplements like milk thistle, but it's essential to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement.

5. **Surgery or Transplant**: In severe cases, surgical intervention or a liver transplant might be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional to discuss your symptoms and get personalized treatment recommendations based on your medical history and condition.
