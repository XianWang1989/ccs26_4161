I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general insights into potential treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice, as they can provide a diagnosis and suitable treatment plan based on your specific condition.

Common treatment options for liver problems may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthier diet (low in fat, high in fruits and vegetables), maintaining a healthy weight, and engaging in regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes, such as antiviral medications for viral hepatitis.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: If alcohol is a contributing factor to liver problems, abstaining from alcohol is essential.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests may be recommended to monitor the progression of liver disease.

5. **Possible Procedures**: In severe cases, procedures like a liver biopsy, endoscopy, or even a liver transplant could be considered, depending on the severity of the liver disease.

6. **Supplementation and Herbal Remedies**: Some may explore milk thistle or other herbal supplements; however, it’s vital to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement.

Remember, only a qualified healthcare professional can provide you with the appropriate treatment tailored to your specific needs and condition. Always seek professional medical advice for health-related issues.
