I'm not a doctor, but common treatment options for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, regular exercise, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests to track progress.

4. **Nutritional Support**: Consulting a dietitian for a personalized meal plan to support liver function.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a tailored approach to your condition.
