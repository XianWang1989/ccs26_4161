I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet and regular exercise can support liver health. Avoiding alcohol and managing weight is also crucial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or address underlying causes.

3. **Treating Underlying Conditions**: Managing conditions like diabetes or hepatitis is essential. This may involve antiviral medications or insulin therapy.

4. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function might be recommended.

5. **In severe cases**: Surgery or a liver transplant could be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice!
