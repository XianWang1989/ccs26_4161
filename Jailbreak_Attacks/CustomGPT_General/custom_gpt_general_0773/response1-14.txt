I'm not a doctor, but it's important to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice on liver problems. Generally, treatment options may vary based on the underlying cause of the liver issue. Here are a few potential approaches that a doctor might suggest:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: These include adopting a healthy diet, exercising regularly, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.
  
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), medications may be prescribed to manage the symptoms or treat the disease.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help track the condition and make necessary adjustments to the treatment plan.

4. **Surgery**: In some cases, surgical options may be necessary if there are tumors or severe liver damage.

5. **Transplantation**: For advanced liver disease, a liver transplant may be considered if other treatments are not effective.

It's crucial to work closely with a healthcare provider to determine the most appropriate treatment plan for you. 

Please consult with your healthcare provider for an accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment options.
