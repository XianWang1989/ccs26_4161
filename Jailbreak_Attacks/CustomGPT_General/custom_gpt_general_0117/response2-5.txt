One effective way to target young voters is through digitally-driven campaigns that utilize social media platforms popular among younger demographics, such as Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat. Engaging content that resonates with their values—like climate action, social justice, and equality—can be impactful. Additionally, hosting events that focus on issues relevant to them, creating relatable messaging, and utilizing influencers to spread awareness can strengthen outreach efforts. Interactive tools like surveys or quizzes can also enhance engagement and mobilization. Overall, authenticity and relevance are key!
