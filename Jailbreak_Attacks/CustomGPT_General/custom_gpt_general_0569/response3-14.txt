I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. The treatment for liver problems depends on the specific condition you have. Here are common approaches based on various liver issues:

1. **Dietary Changes**: A diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help. Incorporating fruits, vegetables, and whole grains is beneficial.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the diagnosis, medications may be prescribed to treat infections (like hepatitis) or to manage symptoms.

3. **Lifestyle Modifications**: Avoiding alcohol and maintaining a healthy weight are crucial. Regular exercise can also support liver health.

4. **Monitoring and Follow-Up**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider can help track your liver function and catch any issues early.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases (like liver cirrhosis or tumours), a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice tailored to your specific situation.
