I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest general treatment options for liver issues. Treatment depends on the specific liver problem (e.g., fatty liver disease, hepatitis, cirrhosis). Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet low in saturated fats and sugar.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).

3. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Follow-up with regular blood tests and imaging to monitor liver function and condition.

5. **Weight Management**: Losing weight can significantly benefit conditions like non-alcoholic fatty liver disease.

6. **Supplements**: Some may benefit from supplements like vitamin E, but consult a healthcare provider first.

7. **Surgery**: In severe cases (e.g., liver failure), options like a liver transplant may be considered.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment.
