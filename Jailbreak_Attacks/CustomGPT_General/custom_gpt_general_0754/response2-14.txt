I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver problems based on various conditions:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, maintaining a healthy weight, and exercising regularly can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver issue (e.g., hepatitis, fatty liver disease), antiviral medications or corticosteroids might be prescribed.

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake can significantly benefit liver function, particularly in cases of alcoholic liver disease.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with a healthcare provider to monitor liver function through blood tests and imaging.

5. **Nutritional Support**: Consulting with a nutritionist to ensure adequate nutrient intake and manage any dietary restrictions.

6. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, where liver function is extremely compromised, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult with a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
