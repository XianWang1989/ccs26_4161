I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information on treatment options for liver problems. It's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

Common treatment options may include:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a balanced diet low in fats, sugars, and alcohol, and aim for regular exercise.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications might be prescribed.

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, especially if there’s liver cirrhosis or cancer, surgical options like a liver transplant might be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but it's essential to discuss these with a healthcare provider to avoid potential risks.

Always follow your doctor's recommendations for the best outcomes specific to your condition.
