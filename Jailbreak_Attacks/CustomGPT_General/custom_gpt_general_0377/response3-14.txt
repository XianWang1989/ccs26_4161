### Possible Treatment Options:
1. **Lifestyle Changes:**
   - **Diet:** Adopt a balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt. Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
   - **Exercise:** Regular physical activity can help improve liver health.
   - **Weight Management:** Achieving and maintaining a healthy weight is essential.

2. **Medications:**
   - Depending on the liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying infections.

3. **Regular Monitoring:**
   - Regular check-ups and lab tests to monitor liver function.

4. **Avoiding Toxins:**
   - Limit alcohol consumption and avoid drugs that can harm the liver.

5. **Surgical Interventions:**
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplant may be recommended.

### Alternative and Complementary Treatments:
- Some people explore herbal supplements or acupuncture, but these should be discussed with a doctor to avoid potential interactions with medications.
