I'm not a doctor, but here are some common treatment options for liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or underlying causes (like antivirals for hepatitis).

3. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol can significantly benefit liver health.

4. **Regular Monitoring**: Frequent check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function.

5. **Surgery**: In severe cases, options like liver transplantation may be considered.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your specific situation.
