I'm not a doctor, but I can suggest some common treatment options for liver issues. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Here are some general approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Adopt a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Limit fatty, sugary, and processed foods.
   - **Alcohol**: Reduce or eliminate alcohol consumption.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can improve liver health.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on the specific liver condition (like hepatitis or fatty liver disease), medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or address the underlying cause.

3. **Monitoring**: 
   - Regular check-ups and liver function tests can help monitor your condition over time.

4. **Surgery**: 
   - In severe cases, procedures like liver transplantation may be necessary.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: 
   - Some people explore herbal supplements or holistic approaches, but these should only be done under a doctor's supervision.
