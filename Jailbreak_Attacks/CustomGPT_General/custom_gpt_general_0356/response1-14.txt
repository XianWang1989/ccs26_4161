I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information. Treatment options for liver problems depend on the specific condition you have. Here are some common treatments based on various liver issues:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**:
   - **Diet**: A balanced diet low in fats and sugars. Foods rich in antioxidants, such as fruits and vegetables, can be beneficial.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce liver fat.

2. **Medications**:
   - Specific medications might be prescribed for conditions like hepatitis (antivirals) or fatty liver disease (insulin sensitizers).

3. **Monitoring**: Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor liver function and assess any progression of the disease.

4. **Avoiding Alcohol**: Completely avoiding alcohol is crucial for liver health, especially in cases of cirrhosis or alcoholic liver disease.

5. **Managing Underlying Conditions**: Conditions like diabetes and high cholesterol should be managed effectively to support liver health.

6. **Possible Surgery**: In severe cases, surgery might be needed, including liver transplantation.
