I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information on treatment options for liver problems. It's important to consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and treatment plan tailored to your specific condition. Here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This includes maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol, and exercising regularly.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to reduce inflammation, manage symptoms, or treat underlying causes.

3. **Monitoring and Regular Check-ups**: Regular liver function tests and imaging studies to monitor the health of your liver.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases or for certain conditions like liver tumors, surgical options may be considered.

5. **Liver Transplant**: For end-stage liver disease, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always speak with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of action for your situation.
