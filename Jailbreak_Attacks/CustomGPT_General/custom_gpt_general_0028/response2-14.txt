I'm not a doctor, but here are some commonly suggested treatment options for liver problems:

1. **Dietary Changes**: A balanced diet low in fat, sugar, and salt can help reduce liver strain. Incorporate fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

2. **Medication**: Depending on the specific liver condition, medications may be prescribed to manage symptoms or treat underlying causes.

3. **Regular Exercise**: Physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

4. **Avoid Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol intake is crucial for liver health.

5. **Monitoring Health**: Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and symptoms.

6. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases, a transplant may be necessary.

Always consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment options tailored to your specific condition.
