I'm not a doctor, but for liver issues, treatment options can vary based on the specific condition. Generally, here are some common approaches:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: Adopt a healthy diet low in saturated fats and sugar, maintain a healthy weight, and avoid alcohol.

2. **Medications**: Depending on the condition, medications like antiviral drugs for hepatitis or supplements for fatty liver disease may be prescribed.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies to monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, procedures such as a liver transplant may be considered.

5. **Alternative Therapies**: Some people explore herbal supplements, but it's crucial to consult a healthcare provider before trying these.

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
