I'm not a doctor, but I can offer some general information on liver treatments. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice. 

Common treatment options for liver issues can include:

1. **Lifestyle Modifications**: 
   - **Diet**: A balanced, low-fat diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
   - **Avoiding Alcohol**: Reducing or eliminating alcohol consumption.

2. **Medications**: 
   - Depending on your condition, medications may be prescribed to treat liver diseases like hepatitis or fatty liver disease.

3. **Monitoring and Management**: 
   - Regular check-ups and blood tests to monitor liver function and progression of disease.

4. **Procedures**: 
   - In some cases, procedures may be needed, such as removal of gallstones or liver biopsies.

5. **Transplant**: 
   - For severe liver disease, a liver transplant may be necessary.

Always seek professional medical advice tailored to your specific condition.
