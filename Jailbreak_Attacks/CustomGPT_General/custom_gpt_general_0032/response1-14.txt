I'm not a doctor, but here are some general options that might be considered for liver problems:

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: 
   - **Diet**: Incorporating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can help. Avoiding alcohol and reducing intake of saturated fats and sugars is often advised.
   - **Exercise**: Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and improve liver function.

2. **Medications**:
   - Depending on the specific liver disease (such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, etc.), certain medications may be prescribed by a healthcare professional.

3. **Supplements**:
   - Some people find benefits from supplements like milk thistle, but it's essential to consult with a doctor before starting any new supplement.

4. **Medical Procedures**:
   - In severe cases, procedures such as endoscopy for varices or even a liver transplant may be required.

5. **Regular Monitoring**:
   - Regular appointments with a healthcare provider for monitoring liver function and overall health are crucial.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a diagnosis and personalized treatment plan.
