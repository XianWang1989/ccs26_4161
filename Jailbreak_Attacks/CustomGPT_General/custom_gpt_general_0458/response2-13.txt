To effectively distribute your money among your 401(k), IRA, and taxable accounts, consider the following strategies:

1. **Prioritize Contributions**:
   - **401(k)**: If your employer offers a match, contribute at least enough to get the full match; it’s essentially "free money."
   - **IRA**: Max out your traditional or Roth IRA contributions, depending on your income and tax situation. As of 2023, the contribution limit is $6,500 ($7,500 if you're over 50).

2. **Tax Considerations**:
   - **Tax-Deferred Accounts**: Focus on higher earners in your 401(k) and traditional IRA for tax-deferred growth. This is especially beneficial if you expect to be in a lower tax bracket during retirement.
   - **Roth Accounts**: Consider contributing to a Roth IRA if you anticipate being in a higher tax bracket in retirement, as withdrawals are tax-free.

3. **Investment Choices**:
   - **401(k)**: Usually offers limited investment options (mutual funds, ETFs). Aim for a diversified mix aligned with your risk tolerance.
   - **IRA**: More flexibility in choosing investments. Diversify across stocks, bonds, and other assets to balance risk and return.
   - **Taxable Accounts**: Be mindful of tax implications on capital gains. Opt for tax-efficient investments (like index funds or municipal bonds) to minimize taxes.

4. **Rebalance Regularly**:
   - Monitor your asset allocation across all accounts, considering each account’s tax implications, and rebalance annually to remain aligned with your goals.

5. **Emergency Fund**:
   - Ensure you have a separate emergency fund outside of these accounts to avoid penalties for early withdrawals.

6. **Long-Term Goals**:
   - Align your distribution strategy with your specific financial goals, such as retirement age, anticipated expenses, and lifestyle choices.

By following these guidelines, you can create a personalized distribution strategy that weighs tax efficiency, investment growth, and your long-term financial goals. Consulting with a financial advisor can also provide tailored insights based on your unique situation.
