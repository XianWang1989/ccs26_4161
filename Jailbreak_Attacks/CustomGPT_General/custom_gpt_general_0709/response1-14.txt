I'm not a doctor, but I can provide some general information about treatment options for common liver problems. 

1. **Lifestyle Changes**: This might include adopting a healthier diet, engaging in regular exercise, avoiding alcohol, and maintaining a healthy weight.

2. **Medications**: Doctors may prescribe medications to address specific liver conditions, such as antivirals for hepatitis or corticosteroids for autoimmune liver diseases.

3. **Regular Monitoring**: Routine blood tests and imaging studies (like ultrasound or CT scans) can help monitor liver function and disease progression.

4. **Liver Transplant**: In severe cases where liver function is critically impaired, a liver transplant may be necessary.

5. **Manage Underlying Conditions**: Treating associated conditions like diabetes or high cholesterol can improve liver health.

Always consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and tailored treatment plan.
