I'm not a doctor, but if you have a liver problem, the best course of action would be to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice. Treatment can vary widely depending on the specific liver condition, so a proper diagnosis is essential. Common approaches include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some severe cases, surgery or liver transplantation. Focus on diet, exercise, and avoiding harmful substances like alcohol. But seriously, just see a doctor; self-diagnosing won't end well.
