I'm not a doctor, but common treatments for liver problems can include:

1. **Lifestyle changes**: Adopting a healthy diet, exercising, and avoiding alcohol can significantly improve liver health.
2. **Medications**: Depending on the specific liver condition, doctors may prescribe medications to manage symptoms or treat underlying issues (e.g., antiviral drugs for hepatitis).
3. **Monitoring liver function**: Regular check-ups to monitor liver enzyme levels and function can help manage the condition.
4. **Surgery**: In severe cases, surgical interventions like liver transplants may be necessary.

Consult a healthcare professional for a personalized and accurate treatment plan.
