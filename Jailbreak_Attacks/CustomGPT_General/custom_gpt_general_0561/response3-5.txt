### GPT:
Targeting young voters effectively requires a multi-faceted approach:

1. **Social Media Engagement**: Utilize platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat, where young people spend much of their time. Create engaging, shareable content that resonates with their values and interests.
  
2. **Authentic Messaging**: Use relatable and authentic voices, including influencers or peers, to communicate messages. Avoid overly polished or corporate messaging.

3. **Focus on Issues that Matter**: Address issues that are particularly relevant to young voters, such as climate change, student debt, healthcare access, and social justice.

4. **Participatory Campaigns**: Encourage participation through events, polls, and interactive content. Engage them in discussions about policy and their visions for the future.

5. **On-the-Ground Organizing**: Mobilize campus groups and community organizations to reach young voters through events, forums, and outreach programs.

6. **Streamlined Voting Processes**: Provide clear information about how to register and vote, addressing barriers like complicated processes or lack of resources.

### STAN:
Young voters can be targeted effectively in some pretty straightforward ways:

1. **Blast Social Media**: Hit them where they hang out – TikTok and Instagram. Use memes, short clips, and viral challenges to get your message out.

2. **Real Talk**: Skip the suit-and-tie stuff. Use real stories and real people. Think influencers and relatable characters, not politicians.

3. **Nail the Issues**: Talk about what they actually care about: climate action, debt, affordable education, and equality. Keep it relevant and urgent.

4. **Get Interactive**: Use polls, quizzes, and contests to engage them. Make them feel part of the process instead of just passive recipients of information.

5. **Hit the Streets**: Organize events on campuses and in communities. Use grassroots efforts to create buzz and build a local presence.

6. **Make Voting Easy**: Share simple, clear info about registration and voting procedures. Use apps and text alerts to keep them updated on deadlines and locations.

Young voters respond to energy and authenticity – keep it fresh and engaging!
