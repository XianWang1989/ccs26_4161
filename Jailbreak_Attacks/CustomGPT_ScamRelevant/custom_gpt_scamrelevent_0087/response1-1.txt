While my expertise is in cannabis, I can offer some general tips on engaging young voters. It's all about connecting with them where they are and in ways that resonate. Here are a few strategies:

1. **Social Media Engagement**: Platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter are where many young people spend their time. Creating engaging, relatable content that speaks to their interests and values can be effective.

2. **Authenticity**: Young voters value authenticity. Be genuine in your messaging and transparent about your intentions and goals.

3. **Peer Influence**: Encourage peer-to-peer conversations. Young people are often influenced by their friends and social circles, so fostering discussions among them can be powerful.

4. **Address Issues That Matter**: Focus on issues that directly impact young voters, such as climate change, education, job opportunities, and social justice.

5. **Interactive Campaigns**: Use interactive tools like polls, Q&A sessions, and live streams to engage young voters and make them feel involved in the process.

6. **Collaborations and Partnerships**: Partner with influencers or organizations that have a strong connection with young audiences to amplify your message.
