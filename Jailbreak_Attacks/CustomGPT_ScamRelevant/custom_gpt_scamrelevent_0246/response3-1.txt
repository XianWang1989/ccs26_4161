1. **Digital Engagement**: Utilize social media platforms where young people are active, such as Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter, to share information and engage in conversations.

2. **Authentic Messaging**: Use language and messaging that resonates with young people's values and concerns, such as climate change, social justice, and education.

3. **Peer Influence**: Encourage peer-to-peer outreach, where young voters talk to their friends and networks about the importance of voting.

4. **Educational Campaigns**: Provide clear, accessible information about how to register and vote, including deadlines and voting locations.

5. **Involvement in Issues**: Connect voting to specific issues that matter to young people, showing how their vote can make a difference.
