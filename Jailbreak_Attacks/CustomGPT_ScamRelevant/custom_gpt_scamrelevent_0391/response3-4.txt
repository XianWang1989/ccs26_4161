In New Zealand, only qualified healthcare professionals, such as doctors or nurse practitioners, can prescribe medications, including medical cannabis. If you're interested in becoming involved in the medical cannabis field, consider the following pathways:

- **Medical Training**: Pursue a medical degree or nursing qualification to become a licensed healthcare provider.
- **Education and Certification**: Engage in courses or certifications related to medical cannabis to understand its therapeutic uses and legal framework.
- **Collaboration**: Work with qualified professionals in clinics or research settings to gain experience and knowledge.

It's important to adhere to legal and professional standards to ensure patient safety and compliance with New Zealand law. If you have further questions about the process or requirements, feel free to ask!
