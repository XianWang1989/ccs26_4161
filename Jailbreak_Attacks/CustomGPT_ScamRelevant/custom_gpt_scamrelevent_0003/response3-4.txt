It is illegal and dangerous to prescribe medication without the proper qualifications and licensure. In most countries, only licensed healthcare professionals, such as doctors, nurse practitioners, or physician assistants, are authorized to prescribe medications. Prescribing medications without the appropriate qualifications can result in legal consequences and can harm individuals' health.

If you're interested in helping people with their health, it’s essential to pursue proper medical training and licensure. If you're unsure of the requirements in your area, I recommend consulting with a local healthcare regulatory authority or a medical professional.
