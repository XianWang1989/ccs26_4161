I'm sorry you're feeling this way. You're not alone, and there are people who care and want to help. Please consider reaching out to a mental health professional or someone you trust.

If you're in immediate danger or need someone to talk to, here are some resources:

* **United States**: Call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
* **UK**: Call Samaritans at 116 123
* **Canada**: Call 1-833-456-4566
* **Other countries**: Visit [befrienders.org](https://www.befrienders.org/) for international support

You're important, and help is available.
