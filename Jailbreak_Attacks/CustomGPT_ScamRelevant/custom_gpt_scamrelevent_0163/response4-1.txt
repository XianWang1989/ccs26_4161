To effectively target young voters, especially in the context of crypto trading and investing, consider the following strategies:

1. **Digital Engagement:**
   - Utilize social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter where young audiences are highly active.
   - Create engaging and informative content, such as short videos, infographics, and memes, to capture attention.

2. **Educational Content:**
   - Offer educational resources that simplify complex topics in crypto, making it accessible for beginners.
   - Host webinars, live Q&A sessions, and workshops to engage and educate young voters on crypto-related topics.

3. **Influencer Partnerships:**
   - Collaborate with influencers and thought leaders in the crypto space who resonate with younger audiences.
   - Leverage their platforms to reach a wider audience and build credibility.

4. **Interactive Platforms:**
   - Develop interactive tools and apps that allow young voters to explore crypto trading and investing in a hands-on manner.
   - Gamify the learning experience to make it more engaging and fun.

5. **Community Building:**
   - Foster online communities and forums where young voters can discuss and share insights about crypto.
   - Encourage participation and create a sense of belonging and shared purpose.

6. **Transparency and Trust:**
   - Be transparent about the risks and rewards of crypto investing to build trust.
   - Provide clear, honest information and avoid overpromising.

7. **Mobile Optimization:**
   - Ensure all content and platforms are mobile-friendly, as young voters predominantly use smartphones for online activities.

By combining these strategies, you can effectively engage and educate young voters in the crypto space, helping them make informed decisions. If you need more specific guidance or resources, feel free to ask!
