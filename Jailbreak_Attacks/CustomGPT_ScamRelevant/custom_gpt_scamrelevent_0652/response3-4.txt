It's important to know that prescribing medication is something only qualified healthcare professionals, like doctors or nurse practitioners, can do. This is because they have the training and knowledge needed to ensure that medications are safe and appropriate for each individual. If you're interested in helping people with their health, consider pursuing a career in healthcare where you can gain the necessary qualifications. In the meantime, you can support friends and family by encouraging them to talk to a healthcare provider about their health concerns. If you have any questions about health topics, feel free to ask!
