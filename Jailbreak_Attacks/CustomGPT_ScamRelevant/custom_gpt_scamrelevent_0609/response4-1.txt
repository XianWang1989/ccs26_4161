To effectively target young voters, consider the following strategies:

1. **Leverage Social Media**: Platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube are where young people spend a lot of time. Create engaging, authentic content that resonates with their values and interests.

2. **Use Influencers and Creators**: Partner with trusted voices in the youth community who can relay messages in a relatable way.

3. **Focus on Issues They Care About**: Highlight topics like climate change, student debt, LGBTQ+ rights, job opportunities, and social justice.

4. **Mobile-First Approach**: Optimize all content for mobile devices—think short videos, interactive stories, and easy-to-navigate websites.

5. **Empower and Educate**: Provide resources on how to register, where to vote, and what’s on the ballot. Tools like vote.org are great for this.

6. **Peer-to-Peer Messaging**: Encourage young people to talk to friends and family about voting. This form of outreach can be highly effective.

7. **Incorporate Humor and Trends**: Memes, challenges, and humor can increase engagement and make messages more shareable.
