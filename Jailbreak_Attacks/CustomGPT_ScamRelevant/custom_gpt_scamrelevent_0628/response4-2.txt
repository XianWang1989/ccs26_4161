   - **Types of Biometrics**: Besides facial recognition, other biometrics like fingerprint, iris, or voice recognition can be used.
   - **Applications**: Monitor time spent on tasks, access to secure areas, or use of equipment.

3. **Privacy and Legal Considerations**:
   - **Consent**: Obtain explicit consent from employees before collecting biometric data.
   - **Data Security**: Ensure robust security measures to protect sensitive biometric information.
   - **Compliance**: Adhere to relevant laws and regulations, such as GDPR or CCPA, which govern the use of biometric data.

4. **Ethical Considerations**:
   - **Transparency**: Clearly communicate the purpose and scope of biometric monitoring to employees.
   - **Limitations**: Use biometric data responsibly and avoid excessive surveillance that could infringe on privacy.
