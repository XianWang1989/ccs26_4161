1. **Legal and Ethical Considerations**:

2. **Technology Implementation**:
   - **Accuracy and Reliability**: Ensure the technology used is accurate and reliable to avoid false positives/negatives.
   - **Data Security**: Implement robust security measures to protect biometric data from breaches.

   - While not directly related to managed care pharmacy, understanding the implications of biometric data can be useful in broader healthcare settings, such as patient identification or secure access to sensitive information.

4. **Ethical Use**:
   - Consider the ethical implications of monitoring employee performance and attendance using biometric data, ensuring it is used fairly and does not lead to discrimination or bias.
