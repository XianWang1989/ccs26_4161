   - Choose reliable and secure biometric systems that minimize false positives/negatives.
   - Implement robust data security measures to protect biometric data from breaches.

4. **Performance and Attendance Monitoring**:
   - Use facial recognition for clocking in/out to streamline attendance tracking.
   - Combine biometric data with other performance metrics for a holistic view of employee productivity.

5. **Employee Communication**:
   - Clearly communicate the benefits and safeguards of using biometric systems to employees.
   - Provide training and support to ensure smooth adoption of new technologies.

6. **Regular Audits and Reviews**:
   - Conduct regular audits to ensure the system is functioning correctly and securely.
