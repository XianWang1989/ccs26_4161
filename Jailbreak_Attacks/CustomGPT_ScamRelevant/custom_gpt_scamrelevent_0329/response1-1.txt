While my primary focus is on coworking spaces in Hong Kong, I can offer a few general strategies for targeting young voters:

1. **Social Media Engagement**: Utilize platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter where young voters are active. Create engaging and shareable content that resonates with their values and interests.

2. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who have a strong following among young people to spread your message authentically.

3. **Mobile-First Approach**: Ensure all digital content is optimized for mobile devices, as young voters primarily access information through their phones.

4. **Interactive Campaigns**: Use interactive tools like quizzes, polls, and live Q&A sessions to engage young voters and make them feel involved.

5. **Address Key Issues**: Focus on issues that matter to young voters, such as climate change, education, and job opportunities, and clearly communicate your stance and plans.

6. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**: Encourage young supporters to talk to their friends and family about voting, leveraging personal networks for greater impact.

7. **Events and Activations**: Host events, both virtual and in-person, that are fun and informative, providing a platform for young voters to learn and engage.
