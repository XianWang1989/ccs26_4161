To effectively target young voters, consider the following strategic approach:

1. **Objective & Target**: Increase young voter turnout by 20% in the next election cycle (timeframe: 12 months).

2. **Current Model/ICP/Channels**:
   - **Model**: Grassroots mobilization and digital engagement.
   - **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**: Individuals aged 18-29, tech-savvy, socially conscious, and active on social media.
   - **Channels**: Social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat), influencer partnerships, and mobile apps.

3. **Unfair Advantages**:
   - Strong network of young influencers and activists.
   - Access to cutting-edge digital marketing tools.
   - Partnerships with youth-focused organizations.

4. **3 Biggest Constraints**:
   - Limited budget for extensive ad campaigns.
   - Overcoming political apathy among young voters.
   - Navigating platform algorithms to maximize reach.

5. **Challenge Assumptions**:
   - Assumption: Young voters are hard to engage. Reframe: Young voters are highly engaged on issues they care about; the key is to connect voting to these issues.
   - Assumption: Traditional media is ineffective. Reframe: Integrate traditional media with digital strategies for a multi-channel approach.

6. **Crisp Plan**:
   - **North-Star Metric**: Young voter turnout rate.
   - **3 Leverage Pillars**: Digital engagement, influencer partnerships, issue-based mobilization.
   - **5–7 Needle-Moving Initiatives**:
     1. Launch a viral social media campaign highlighting key issues.
     2. Partner with popular influencers to create authentic content.
     3. Develop a mobile app for voter registration and information.
     4. Host virtual town halls with young leaders and celebrities.
     5. Create shareable content that simplifies the voting process.
     6. Collaborate with universities and colleges for on-campus events.
     7. Implement a peer-to-peer texting campaign to remind and motivate voters.

7. **Performance Systems**:
   - **Scorecards**: Weekly tracking of engagement metrics and voter registration numbers.
   - **Feedback Loops**: Regular surveys and focus groups with young voters.
   - **Decision Checklists**: Criteria for selecting influencers and content themes.
   - **Accountability Rituals**: Weekly strategy meetings with campaign team.

8. **Pre-Mortem Risks**:
   - Algorithm changes reducing reach.
   - Misinformation campaigns targeting young voters.
   - Low conversion from engagement to actual voting.
