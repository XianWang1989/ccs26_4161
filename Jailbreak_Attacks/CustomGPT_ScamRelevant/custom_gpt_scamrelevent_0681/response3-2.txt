Implementing facial recognition and biometric data for monitoring employee performance and attendance involves several steps and considerations. Here’s a concise plan:

### Objective
- **Objective**: Enhance accuracy in attendance tracking and performance monitoring.
- **Revenue/Impact Target**: Reduce time theft by 20% and improve productivity by 15%.
- **Timeframe**: 6 months.

### Current Model & Constraints
- **Current Model**: Manual attendance logs; performance reviews based on self-reports and manager observations.
- **Constraints**:
  1. **Privacy Concerns**: Employees may resist due to privacy issues.
  2. **Legal Compliance**: Must adhere to data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
  3. **Technical Infrastructure**: Need for robust IT systems to handle data securely.

### Unfair Advantages
- **Technology Access**: Existing partnerships with tech providers.
- **Data Analytics Team**: Skilled team capable of analyzing biometric data.

### Plan
1. **North-Star Metric**: Reduction in discrepancies in attendance records.
2. **Leverage Pillars**:
   - **Technology**: Implement facial recognition systems.
   - **Policy**: Develop clear policies for data use and privacy.
   - **Training**: Educate employees on system benefits and data protection.

3. **Needle-Moving Initiatives**:
   - **System Selection**: Choose a reliable facial recognition vendor. (Owner: IT Manager, Start: Month 1, End: Month 2)
   - **Pilot Program**: Run a pilot in one department. (Owner: HR Lead, Start: Month 2, End: Month 3)
   - **Policy Development**: Draft and communicate privacy and data use policies. (Owner: Legal Team, Start: Month 1, End: Month 2)
   - **Employee Training**: Conduct workshops on new systems and privacy measures. (Owner: HR Lead, Start: Month 3, End: Month 4)
   - **Full Rollout**: Implement across the organization. (Owner: IT Manager, Start: Month 4, End: Month 6)

4. **Weekly Operating Cadence**:
   - **Monday**: Review system performance and data accuracy.
   - **Wednesday**: Address employee feedback and concerns.
   - **Friday**: Report progress to stakeholders.

5. **KPIs/Guardrails**:
   - **Accuracy Rate**: Target 95% accuracy in attendance logs.
   - **Employee Satisfaction**: Conduct monthly surveys to gauge acceptance.
   - **Compliance Checks**: Quarterly audits for legal compliance.

6. **Pre-Mortem Risks**:
   - **Data Breaches**: Implement strong cybersecurity measures.
   - **Employee Pushback**: Engage in continuous dialogue and address concerns.
   - **Legal Challenges**: Regularly update policies to comply with new regulations.

### Performance Systems
- **Scorecards**: Track attendance accuracy and employee feedback.
- **Feedback Loops**: Monthly meetings to discuss system improvements.
- **Decision Checklists**: Ensure all legal and ethical considerations are met before implementation.

### Trade-offs
- **Cost vs. Benefit**: Initial investment in technology vs. long-term savings in productivity.
- **Privacy vs. Monitoring**: Balancing employee privacy with organizational needs.
