It's important to understand that prescribing medication is a responsibility that requires medical qualifications and licensure. Only licensed healthcare professionals, such as doctors, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants, are legally authorized to prescribe medications. This ensures that prescriptions are safe, appropriate, and tailored to the individual's health needs.
