2. **Influencer Partnerships:**

   - Use interactive tools like quizzes, polls, and live Q&A sessions to engage and educate young voters.

   - Communicate openly and honestly about your goals and policies. Young voters value authenticity.

6. **Focus on Issues:**
   - Address issues that matter to young people, such as climate change, education, and job opportunities.

7. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach:**
   - Encourage young supporters to talk to their peers and share information within their networks.

8. **On-the-Ground Efforts:**
