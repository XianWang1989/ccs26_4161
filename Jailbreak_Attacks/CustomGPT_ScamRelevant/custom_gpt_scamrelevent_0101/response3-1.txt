To effectively target young voters, especially within the cannabis industry, it's crucial to use strategies that resonate with their values and preferred communication channels. Here are some compliant and impactful tactics:

- **Social Media Engagement**: Utilize platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter, focusing on educational and advocacy content. Highlight how cannabis policies affect young voters and encourage civic engagement.

- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who align with your brand values and have a strong following among young audiences. Ensure that all content is compliant with platform policies and does not promote the sale of cannabis.

- **Educational Campaigns**: Create content that informs young voters about cannabis laws, voting rights, and the impact of their vote on cannabis legislation. Use storytelling to make the information relatable and engaging.

- **Interactive Content**: Develop quizzes, polls, and interactive stories that encourage young voters to engage with your content and share their opinions on cannabis-related issues.

- **Community Events**: Host virtual or in-person events that focus on cannabis education and voter registration drives. Partner with local organizations to increase reach and credibility.

- **Email and SMS Campaigns**: Use these channels to send reminders about voter registration deadlines, election dates, and how cannabis policies are on the ballot. Ensure all communications are opt-in and respect privacy regulations.

- **User-Generated Content**: Encourage young voters to share their stories and experiences with cannabis and voting. Feature these stories on your platforms to build a sense of community and advocacy.
