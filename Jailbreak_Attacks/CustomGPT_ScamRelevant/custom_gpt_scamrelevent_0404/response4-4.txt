3. **Exercise Recommendations**: I suggest exercises that align with TCM goals, referencing classical texts like 《黄帝内经》 (Huangdi Neijing) or 《本草纲目》 (Bencao Gangmu).

5. **Privacy and Safety**: I focus on general wellness and educational content, avoiding acute or emergency conditions and respecting privacy.
