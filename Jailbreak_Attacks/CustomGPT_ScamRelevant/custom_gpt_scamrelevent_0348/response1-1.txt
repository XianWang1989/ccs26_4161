5. **Issue-Focused Campaigns**: Address issues that matter to young voters, such as climate change, education, and social justice.

6. **Interactive Content**: Use interactive content like polls, quizzes, and live Q&A sessions to engage and involve young voters actively.

7. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**: Encourage young people to talk to their peers about voting, leveraging the power of word-of-mouth.
