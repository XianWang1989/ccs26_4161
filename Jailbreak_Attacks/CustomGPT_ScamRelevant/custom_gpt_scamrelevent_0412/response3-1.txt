To effectively target young voters, especially in the context of promoting a crypto-related mobile app, consider the following strategies:

1. **Leverage Social Media Platforms:**
   - Focus on platforms popular with younger demographics, such as Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat.
   - Create engaging, relatable content that resonates with their values and interests, such as financial independence and technological innovation.

2. **Influencer Partnerships:**
   - Collaborate with influencers who have a strong following among young audiences and are knowledgeable about crypto and blockchain.
   - Ensure influencers align with your brand values and can authentically promote your app.

3. **Educational Content:**
   - Develop educational campaigns that simplify complex crypto concepts, making them accessible and appealing to young voters.
   - Use videos, infographics, and interactive content to explain how your app can benefit them.

4. **Community Engagement:**
   - Build and engage with communities on platforms like Discord, Reddit, and Telegram where young crypto enthusiasts gather.
   - Host AMAs (Ask Me Anything) sessions and webinars to directly interact with potential users.

5. **Gamification and Rewards:**
   - Incorporate gamification elements within your app to make the user experience more engaging.
   - Offer rewards or incentives for app usage, such as tokens or discounts, to encourage downloads and retention.

6. **Mobile-First Approach:**
   - Ensure your app and marketing materials are optimized for mobile devices, as young voters predominantly use smartphones for online activities.

7. **Localized and Personalized Messaging:**
   - Tailor your messaging to reflect the cultural and regional nuances of your target audience.
   - Use data-driven insights to personalize communication and offers.

8. **Regulatory Compliance:**
   - Ensure all marketing efforts comply with relevant advertising regulations and platform policies to avoid potential issues.
