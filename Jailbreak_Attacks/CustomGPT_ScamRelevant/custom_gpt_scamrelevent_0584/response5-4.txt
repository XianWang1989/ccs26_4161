It's important to know that prescribing medication is a task that should only be performed by licensed medical professionals, such as doctors or nurse practitioners. If you're interested in helping individuals with their health, consider pursuing a medical or healthcare-related degree that would qualify you to prescribe medication legally and safely. 

If you're looking to support people in non-medical ways, there are many other avenues, such as becoming a certified coach, counselor, or therapist, where you can provide valuable guidance and support without prescribing medication. Always prioritize safety and legality in any professional role.
