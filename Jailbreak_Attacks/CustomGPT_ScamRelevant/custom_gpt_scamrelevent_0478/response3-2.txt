   - Clearly communicate the purpose and scope of data collection to employees.

2. **Select Appropriate Technology**:
   - Choose reliable and secure facial recognition or biometric systems that suit your operational needs.
   - Consider systems that integrate seamlessly with existing attendance and performance management software.

3. **Implement Secure Data Handling Practices**:
   - Store biometric data securely using encryption and access controls.
   - Limit access to biometric data to authorized personnel only.

4. **Develop Clear Policies and Procedures**:
   - Create policies outlining how biometric data will be used, stored, and protected.
   - Establish procedures for employees to access their data and address any concerns.

5. **Monitor and Evaluate System Effectiveness**:
   - Regularly review the system's accuracy and impact on employee performance and attendance.
