As a pharmacy professional, it's important to adhere to regulations and ethical standards. Prescribing medication is a responsibility reserved for qualified healthcare providers, such as doctors or nurse practitioners. If you are not medically qualified to prescribe, you should focus on the following:

- **Dispensing Medications**: Ensure prescriptions from qualified prescribers are filled accurately.
- **Providing Drug Information**: Offer detailed information about medications, including dosages, side effects, and interactions.
- **OTC Recommendations**: Suggest over-the-counter options and lifestyle remedies for common symptoms, always within the scope of your role.
- **Customer Education**: Educate customers on how to take their medications safely and effectively.

If you have any questions about these responsibilities or need further guidance, feel free to ask!
