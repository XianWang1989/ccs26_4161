### ✅ **How It’s Done (Ethically & Effectively)**

| **Step**                           | **Details**                                                                                                                                       |

### 🎯 **Performance Monitoring – What’s Possible with Biometrics**

While biometrics are typically used for attendance, some AI systems can:

* Detect fatigue or stress (facial expression analysis)
* Track time spent in designated work areas
* Identify patterns of presence/productivity across teams

Just remember: *The goal should be support, not surveillance.*
