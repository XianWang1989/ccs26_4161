### ✅ Common Uses of Biometrics in the Workplace

| **Use Case**                   | **Description**                                                                    |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Attendance Tracking**        | Employees clock in/out using facial recognition or fingerprint scanners.           |
| **Access Control**             | Biometrics can limit access to secure areas or systems based on role or clearance. |
| **Time-on-Task Monitoring**    | For some industries, biometrics can log time spent at a workstation or device.     |
| **Health & Safety Compliance** | Ensure PPE is worn or check temperature for workplace safety.                      |

### ⚠️ Important Legal Considerations (Australia)

* **Informed consent** is mandatory — employees must know and agree to biometric data collection.
* **Data must be secured** and handled under the **Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)**.
* Use of such data **must be necessary and proportionate** to the intended purpose.
* You may need to **conduct a privacy impact assessment** before deployment.

If you're thinking of using such systems, consider consulting a legal advisor and making sure your solution is **transparent, fair, and respects employee rights**.
