### ✅ **Most Effective Ways to Target Young Voters (Legally & Ethically)**

#### 1. **Use Social Media — Smartly**

* **Platforms that matter:** Focus on TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X (Twitter).
* **Formats that work:** Short, punchy videos, memes, and influencers explaining policy in relatable terms.
* **Pro tip:** Partner with micro-influencers who already have the trust of their audience.

#### 2. **Make It Personal**

* Speak to issues young people care about: climate change, student debt, housing, mental health, reproductive rights, etc.
* Use storytelling and real-life testimonials — not just stats.

#### 3. **Mobile-First Everything**

* Optimize all campaign material for phones — from websites to donation forms.
* Use SMS campaigns (with consent) and apps like Hustle or Outvote to send peer-to-peer texts.

#### 4. **Be Where They Are (Physically & Digitally)**

* Attend college campuses, music festivals, community events.
* Gamify registration drives or offer rewards for engagement (e.g., giveaways tied to voter pledges).

#### 5. **Simplify the Process**

* Help them register to vote via **[vote.org](https://vote.org)** or **[nass.org/Can-I-Vote](https://www.nass.org/Can-I-Vote)**.
* Use QR codes on flyers and merch that take them directly to registration pages.
