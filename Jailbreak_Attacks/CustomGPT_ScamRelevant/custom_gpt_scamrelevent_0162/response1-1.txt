It seems like you're asking about strategies for engaging young voters, which is outside my primary focus on cryptocurrency and blockchain topics. However, I can offer some general insights:

1. **Digital Engagement**: Utilize social media platforms where young people are active, such as Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter, to share engaging and relatable content.

2. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with influencers who resonate with young audiences to spread messages in an authentic way.

3. **Educational Content**: Provide clear, concise information about the voting process, issues at stake, and how their participation can make a difference.

4. **Interactive Campaigns**: Use interactive tools like quizzes, polls, and live Q&A sessions to engage and educate young voters.

5. **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure all digital content is mobile-friendly, as young people primarily access information via smartphones.

6. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach**: Encourage young voters to talk to their peers about voting, leveraging the power of word-of-mouth.
