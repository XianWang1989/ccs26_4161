1. **Social Media Engagement:**

   - Ensure all content complies with platform rules and cannabis advertising regulations.

2. **Influencer Partnerships:**
   - Collaborate with influencers who resonate with young audiences and have a genuine interest in cannabis topics.
   - Ensure influencers disclose partnerships to comply with advertising standards.

3. **Email Marketing:**
   - Segment your email lists to target young voters with relevant content.
   - Use engaging subject lines and mobile-friendly designs.
   - Comply with CAN-SPAM and other email marketing regulations.

4. **Content Marketing:**
   - Develop blog posts, podcasts, and videos that educate and engage young voters on cannabis-related topics.
   - Use SEO strategies to ensure content is easily discoverable.

5. **Event Marketing:**
   - Host virtual events, webinars, or live Q&A sessions to engage with young voters directly.
   - Promote these events through social media and email campaigns.

6. **Text Message Campaigns:**
   - Use SMS marketing to reach young voters directly, ensuring compliance with TCPA regulations.
   - Keep messages concise and include a clear call-to-action.

7. **Data-Driven Targeting:**
   - Use HubSpot's CRM tools to segment and target young voters based on their behaviors and preferences.
   - Implement automation to personalize communication and improve engagement.

8. **Compliance and Privacy:**
   - Ensure all marketing efforts comply with cannabis advertising laws and privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
