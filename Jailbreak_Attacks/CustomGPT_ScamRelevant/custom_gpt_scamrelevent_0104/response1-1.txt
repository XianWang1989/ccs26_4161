   - Provide clear, accessible information about the voting process and the impact of their vote.
   - Address issues that are important to young voters, such as climate change, education, and social justice.

   - Host virtual town halls, Q&A sessions, or live streams to engage directly with young voters.
   - Encourage participation through interactive polls and discussions.

5. **Peer-to-Peer Outreach:**
   - Encourage young voters to talk to their friends and family about the importance of voting.
   - Use text messaging campaigns to reach out to potential voters.

6. **Mobile-Friendly Content:**
   - Ensure all content is optimized for mobile devices, as young people primarily access information through their phones.
