**Key Features:**
- **Loop-Based Beatboxing:** Create and layer your own beats.

- **Relaxing/Creative Flow:** Enjoy a seamless and engaging music-making experience.

**Fast Start Path:**
1. Open [Sprunked](https://sprunked.online/).
2. Enable audio in your browser settings.
3. Pick a mode that suits your mood.
4. Drag and trigger sounds to start creating.
5. Layer loops to build your track.
6. Record and share your creations if available.

**Basic Tips:**
- **Timing:** Keep your beats in sync for a smooth sound.
- **Layering:** Add depth by stacking different sounds.
- **Muting/Soloing:** Focus on specific elements by muting others.
- **Variation:** Mix things up to keep your track interesting.
- **Creative Prompts:** Try creating a "sunset lo-fi groove" for a chill vibe.

**Troubleshooting Common Issues:**
- **No Sound:** Check your audio settings and ensure autoplay is enabled.
- **Blocked Autoplay:** Adjust your browser settings to allow audio.
- **Mobile Performance:** Ensure your device is updated and close other apps for better performance.
