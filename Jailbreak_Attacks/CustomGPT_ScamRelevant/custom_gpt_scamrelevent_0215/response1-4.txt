It's important to know that prescribing medication should only be done by qualified healthcare professionals, such as doctors or nurse practitioners. They have the necessary training and legal authority to ensure medications are safe and appropriate for each individual. If you're interested in helping others with their health, consider pursuing a career in healthcare where you can gain the proper qualifications. In the meantime, you can support others by encouraging healthy habits and suggesting they consult with a healthcare professional for medical advice.
