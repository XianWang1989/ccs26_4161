To effectively engage young voters, it's important to connect with them authentically and through the channels they frequent. Here's a compact game plan:

1. **Opener or Engagement Strategy:**
   - **Social Media Campaigns:** Use platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat to create visually engaging content that resonates with their values and interests.
   - **Influencer Partnerships:** Collaborate with influencers who have a strong connection with young audiences to share your message in a relatable way.
   - **Interactive Content:** Create polls, quizzes, or challenges that encourage participation and shareability.

2. **Follow-up Thread:**
   - **Question:** "What issues matter most to you in this election?"
   - **Value Add:** Share informative content about how these issues are being addressed and how young voters can make an impact.

3. **Permission-Based Escalation:**
   - "If you're interested, we'd love to invite you to a virtual town hall where you can ask questions directly to the candidates."

4. **Graceful Exit if Signals are Weak:**
   - "Thanks for engaging with us! Feel free to reach out anytime if you have more questions or want to get involved."

**Coach Mindset:**
- Focus on curiosity and genuine interest in their perspectives.
- Mirror their energy and communication style.
- Note signals of engagement or disinterest to adjust strategies accordingly.

**Question for Tailoring Next Steps:**
What platforms or types of content do you think resonate most with your target audience?
